# Assault Shooting

## Summary

Assault Shooting is the shooting mode that lets an unengaged unit that advanced still shoot, but only with ASSAULT weapons.

## Definition

An unengaged unit that advanced this turn and has one or more ASSAULT weapons may shoot, but only with ASSAULT weapons.

## Rules

Assault Shooting is an eligible shooting type used when a unit is selected to [[Shoot]].

The unit can shoot after advancing only through this shooting mode.

Only ASSAULT weapons can be used for the attacks made through this permission.

After shooting, the unit cannot start an action until the end of the phase.

## Interactions

- [[Advance Move]] supplies the movement-state condition that normally blocks shooting unless this exception applies.
- [[Shoot]] is the parent procedure that consumes this shooting permission.
- [[Weapon System]] is the published neighbouring domain that owns weapon-side data such as whether a weapon has the ASSAULT ability.
- The attacks themselves still resolve through the shared attack pipeline defined outside the Shooting domain.

## Restrictions

- The unit must be unengaged.
- The unit must have advanced this turn.
- The unit may only shoot with ASSAULT weapons.
- This article does not define weapon abilities, attack resolution, or movement procedures beyond the condition it consumes.
