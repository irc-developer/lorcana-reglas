# Shooting at Engaged Monsters and Vehicles

## Summary

Shooting at Engaged Monsters and Vehicles is the targeting exception that allows ranged attacks to target enemy engaged MONSTER and VEHICLE units in the Shooting phase.

## Definition

In your Shooting phase, enemy engaged MONSTER and VEHICLE units can be targeted by ranged attacks.

## Rules

This rule is a shooting-side targeting exception used within the Shooting phase.

Attacks against such units are made at -1 to hit, except CLOSE-QUARTERS attacks made by models in a unit engaged with the target.

Being engaged with a MONSTER or VEHICLE does not itself make the attacking unit eligible to shoot.

The attacking unit still needs an applicable shooting permission, such as [[Close-quarters Shooting]].

## Interactions

- [[Close-quarters Shooting]] supplies one of the shooting permissions that can combine with this target exception.
- [[Shoot]] remains the parent procedure that determines whether the unit is actually resolving shooting.
- [[Attack System]] consumes the resulting target legality once shooting is permitted.
- This article narrows neither engagement rules nor weapon-based restrictions that are owned by neighbouring domains.

## Restrictions

- This article only grants target eligibility against enemy engaged MONSTER and VEHICLE units in your Shooting phase.
- It does not create a standalone permission for an engaged unit to shoot.
- Other weapon-based or attack-based restrictions still apply.
