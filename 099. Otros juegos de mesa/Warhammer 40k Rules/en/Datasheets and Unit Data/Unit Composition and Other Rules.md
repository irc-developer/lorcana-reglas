# Unit Composition and Other Rules

## Summary

Unit Composition and Other Rules is the datasheet section that records the models in the unit and the remaining unit-specific rules text carried by that entry.

## Definition

This datasheet section identifies the unit's composition and the other rules text grouped with that data on the unit entry.

## Rules

The section records which models make up the unit and the additional unit-facing rules text presented with that composition data.

It is read with the rest of the datasheet rather than as a separate mechanical system.

## Interactions

- [[Datasheets]] is the parent container that carries this section.
- [[Datasheet Name]] is adjacent because unit identity and unit composition are read together.
- [[Abilities]] is adjacent because some unit-facing rules text on the datasheet intersects with the wider ability model.
- [[Wargear Options]] is adjacent because composition and equipment choices are often read together on the same unit entry.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the datasheet section, not every specific rule that may appear inside a given unit entry.
- This article does not replace the separate ownership of abilities, weapons, keywords, or profiles.
