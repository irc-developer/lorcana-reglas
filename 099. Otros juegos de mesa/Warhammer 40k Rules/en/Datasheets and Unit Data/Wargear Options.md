# Wargear Options

## Summary

Wargear Options is the datasheet section that records the selectable equipment changes available to a unit.

## Definition

This datasheet section lists the equipment choices that can modify how the unit is equipped.

## Rules

Wargear Options records which equipment changes a unit can make from its datasheet.

Those options can affect the weapons or other equipment the unit uses, but the datasheet section itself remains a unit-data surface rather than a separate rules domain.

## Interactions

- [[Datasheets]] is the parent container that carries this section.
- [[Weapons]] is adjacent because equipment choices can change the weapons the unit has.
- [[Unit Composition and Other Rules]] is adjacent because composition and equipment choices are read together on the unit entry.
- [[Weapon System]] is the wider neighbouring domain when the question becomes about weapon rules rather than datasheet options.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the datasheet section, not the full rules meaning of every item of wargear.
- This article does not replace the wider weapon, ability, or keyword domains consumed by specific options.
