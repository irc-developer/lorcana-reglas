# Weapons

## Summary

Weapons is the datasheet surface that records the weapons a unit can use and routes readers into the wider weapon model.

## Definition

The weapons section of a datasheet identifies the weapons associated with the unit.

## Rules

The datasheet lists the weapons available to the unit in its weapons section.

That section is a unit-data surface and should be read together with profiles and wargear options when the question is which weapons the unit has or can take.

The section does not replace the separate rules meaning of weapon profiles, weapon abilities, or the wider weapon system.

## Interactions

- [[Datasheets]] is the parent container that carries the weapons section.
- [[Profiles]] is adjacent because weapon entries and profile lines are read together.
- [[Wargear Options]] is adjacent because datasheet choices can change which weapons the unit uses.
- [[Weapon System]] owns the wider weapon model and the rules meaning of weapon-facing concepts beyond the datasheet container itself.

## Restrictions

- This article is about the datasheet section named Weapons, not the entire rules domain of weapons.
- This article does not redefine weapon profiles, weapon abilities, or attack procedures.
