# Datasheet Name

## Summary

Datasheet Name is the identifying name of the unit entry shown on a datasheet.

## Definition

The datasheet name identifies which unit the datasheet describes.

## Rules

The datasheet name is the primary identifying label for the unit entry.

It distinguishes the datasheet as a specific unit record before the reader moves into profiles, abilities, weapons, composition, or wargear options.

## Interactions

- [[Datasheets]] is the parent container that carries the datasheet name.
- [[Unit Composition and Other Rules]] is a neighbouring datasheet surface because the unit identity and the models included in the unit are read together.

## Restrictions

- This article identifies the title surface of the datasheet; it does not replace the rest of the unit data.
- This article does not absorb naming decisions from codex-specific presentation outside the current Core Rules scope.
