# Scout Move

## Summary

Scout Move is the pre-battle movement procedure that lets an eligible unit reposition during the Resolve Pre-battle Abilities step before the battle begins.

## Definition

During the Resolve Pre-battle Abilities step, a unit wholly within its deployment zone can move up to the X in [[Scouts]] X" by making a scout move.

## Rules

The unit must be wholly within its [[Deployment zone]] when the move begins.

The unit can move up to the X value granted by [[Scouts]].

After moving, the unit must be more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units.

[[Scouts]] can also move eligible units from strategic reserves into the deployment zone.

[[Scouts]] can also move a DEDICATED TRANSPORT if all embarked models have [[Scouts]].

## Interactions

- [[Moving Units]] provides the baseline move-handling logic that still applies unless the pre-battle timing rule changes it.
- [[Deployment zone]] defines the required starting area for the on-battlefield version of the procedure.
- [[Strategic Reserves System]] is the adjacent system for the reserve-state branch that [[Scouts]] can move into the deployment zone.
- [[Scouts]] is the ability that grants this pre-battle procedure.
- This procedure happens during Resolve Pre-battle Abilities, after Declare Battle Formations and before Begin the Battle, which is why it interacts differently with transport-related procedures than in-battle moves do.

## Restrictions

- The unit must begin the move wholly within its deployment zone unless the rule is using the reserve-entry branch.
- The unit must finish more than 8" horizontally from all enemy units.
- A unit cannot embark within a transport after making a scout move because of the pre-battle timing window in which this procedure happens.
