# Moving Monsters and Vehicles

## Summary

Moving Monsters and Vehicles is the movement override that lets MONSTER and VEHICLE models pass through most other models while making a normal or advance move.

## Definition

When making a normal or advance move, MONSTER and VEHICLE models can move through friendly and enemy models except other MONSTER or VEHICLE models.

## Rules

The rule applies during a [[Normal Move]] or an [[Advance Move]].

Eligible models can move through friendly models.

Eligible models can also move through enemy models.

They cannot move through other MONSTER or VEHICLE models.

This is a specific exception layered onto the baseline movement rules from [[Moving Units]].

## Interactions

- [[Moving Units]] remains the baseline procedure for distance, final legality, and all movement conditions not explicitly changed here.
- [[Normal Move]] and [[Advance Move]] are the two movement-phase branches that this exception modifies.
- [[Flying Models]] is a different override that also changes what an eligible unit can move through, but it operates through taking to the skies rather than through the MONSTER or VEHICLE keywords.

## Restrictions

- This override applies only to normal and advance moves.
- It does not permit movement through other MONSTER or VEHICLE models.
- It does not remove terrain, coherency, or end-of-move legality requirements.
