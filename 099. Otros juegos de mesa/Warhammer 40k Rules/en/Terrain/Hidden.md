# Hidden

## Summary

Hidden is the terrain-derived visibility state for qualifying INFANTRY, BEASTS, and SWARM models within dense terrain areas that have not recently made ranged attacks.

## Definition

A model is hidden if it is INFANTRY, BEASTS, or SWARM within a [[Terrain area]] containing one or more dense terrain features and its unit did not make ranged attacks during this turn or the previous turn.

## Rules

A hidden model can only be visible to enemy models within its [[Detection range]].

[[Detection range]] is 15 inches unless another rule states otherwise.

Hidden converts terrain and recent battlefield activity into a visibility restriction consumed by other systems.

## Interactions

- [[Terrain and Visibility]] is the parent terrain umbrella for hidden state.
- [[Terrain area]] and dense terrain are the terrain-side prerequisites for becoming hidden.
- [[Visibility]] remains the baseline rule that Hidden narrows.
- [[Attack System]] consumes the resulting visible-or-not-visible state but does not own the Hidden condition itself.

## Restrictions

- Hidden only applies to the listed model types and only while the unit satisfies the no-ranged-attacks condition.
- Hidden does not redefine shooting permission or attack sequence procedure.
- Other non-terrain visibility exceptions remain external and are not redefined here.
