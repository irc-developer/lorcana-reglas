# Benefit of Cover

## Summary

Benefit of Cover is the terrain-derived defensive state that a target can gain against ranged attacks when terrain or obscuring conditions prevent full visibility.

## Definition

A unit has the benefit of cover against a ranged attack if every model in it either is INFANTRY, BEASTS, or SWARM within a [[Terrain area]] or is not [[Fully visible]] because of intervening terrain and or intervening obscuring terrain areas.

## Rules

If a target has the benefit of cover, worsen the BS characteristic of that attack by 1.

The cover test is unit-wide: every model in the target unit must qualify through terrain area placement or lack of full visibility.

Terrain supplies the battlefield state, while the attack system consumes that state during ranged attacks.

Other rules can grant or ignore benefit of cover, but those rules remain external to this article unless they are terrain-owned.

## Interactions

- [[Terrain and Visibility]] is the parent terrain umbrella for this cover state.
- [[Terrain area]], [[Fully visible]], and [[Obscuring]] provide the terrain-side conditions that determine whether cover applies.
- [[Attack System]] consumes benefit of cover during ranged combat, but Terrain owns the state itself rather than the attack procedure.

## Restrictions

- Benefit of Cover applies against ranged attacks; this article does not grant target eligibility or resolve the attack sequence.
- A target unit does not have benefit of cover unless every model in it satisfies one of the listed conditions.
- Non-terrain rules that grant, ignore, or modify cover remain outside the ownership of this page.
