# Exposed

## Summary

Exposed is the terrain category for terrain that offers minimal protection and no terrain-derived movement hindrance.

## Definition

Exposed terrain offers minimal protection and can be traversed without hindrance.

## Rules

Examples given in the rules include craters, razorwire, and scattered debris.

All models can move horizontally and vertically through exposed terrain features.

Exposed terrain remains part of the battlefield without creating the stronger cover and blocking effects used by other terrain categories.

## Interactions

- [[Terrain Categories]] places Exposed within the terrain classification model.
- [[Movement System]] consumes this category when determining how terrain affects route selection and traversal.
- [[Light]] is the neighbouring published category that adds further terrain outcomes beyond Exposed.

## Restrictions

- Exposed does not itself create benefit of cover, hidden state, or solid blocking.
- This article does not define how to perform a move; it only states the terrain effect that movement procedures consume.
- Exposed is still terrain and must not be confused with open ground that lacks a terrain feature altogether.
