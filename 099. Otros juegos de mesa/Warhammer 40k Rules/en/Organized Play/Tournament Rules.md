# Tournament Rules

> This article describes tournament or organized play procedures. It does not replace the Warhammer 40,000 Core Rules.

## Summary

The Warhammer Event Companion provides an official event framework using the latest Chapter Approved Mission Deck. Games Workshop events generally follow it in full, but organizers can adapt it to local circumstances.

## Scope

This article explains the status and boundaries of the event framework. Detailed mission, scoring, pairing, and specialist-format procedures are covered in linked articles.

It does not supply policies for clocks, concessions, drops, byes, proxies, conversions, WYSIWYG, judge appeals, or penalties because version 1.0 does not define them.

## Practical Procedure

1. Confirm which event companion applies: general, Doubles, Teams, or Dominatus.
2. Use the most recent Chapter Approved Mission Deck and the Warhammer 40,000 app resources specified by the pack.
3. Follow the published event mission sequence and any organizer adaptations.
4. Keep event-only procedures separate from the Core Rules.

The general companion does not cover Doubles, Teams, or Dominatus. Each of those formats uses its own companion alongside the general document.

## Player Impact

Players must read the event pack rather than assume that every tournament uses an identical adaptation. They should bring the required mission resources and record event-specific choices on their roster.

## Organizer Impact

Organizers should state which recommendations are being used and publish local adaptations before the event.
