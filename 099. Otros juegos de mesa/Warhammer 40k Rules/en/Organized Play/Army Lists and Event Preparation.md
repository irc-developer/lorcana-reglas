# Army Lists and Event Preparation

> This article describes tournament or organized play procedures. It does not replace the Warhammer 40,000 Core Rules.

## Summary

Event preparation starts with mustering an army through the Warhammer 40,000 app and recording the event choices required by the applicable companion.

## Scope

This article covers roster information and format-specific preparation stated in the PDFs. It does not invent submission deadlines, file formats, source cut-offs, proxy policy, or modelling standards.

## Practical Procedure

- Muster the army as directed by the Warhammer 40,000 app.
- Select one available [[Force Dispositions|Force Disposition]] and record it on the roster.
- Bring access to the current Chapter Approved Mission Deck.
- For [[Doubles Events]], apply team-wide uniqueness restrictions and record the team's Warlord and Force Disposition.
- For [[Team Events]], the captain ensures lists are submitted on time and scores are submitted after each round.
- For [[Dominatus Events]], record the selected Alliance and Force Disposition and bring a Dominatus deck.

The general companion does not specify a universal list deadline or a closed list of permitted publications. Those details remain event-pack decisions.

## Player Impact

Players should verify their roster contains every event-specific choice before arrival. Specialist formats can make an otherwise legal individual list invalid at team level.

## Organizer Impact

Organizers should publish deadlines and source cut-offs separately because version 1.0 does not provide them. Team captains need a clear method for list and score submission.
