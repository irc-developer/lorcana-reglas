# Missions and Scoring

> This article describes tournament or organized play procedures. It does not replace the Warhammer 40,000 Core Rules.

## Summary

Event games combine one Primary Mission with either Fixed or Tactical Secondary Missions and a Battle Ready score, for a normal maximum of 100 VP.

## Scope

This article covers mission choice, secondary-mission handling, scoring caps, and the Chapter Approved clarifications printed in version 1.0. It excludes layouts and objective-placement diagrams.

## Practical Procedure

### Primary Mission

Each player finds the opponent's Force Disposition symbol on their own Force Disposition card. The listed mission is that player's Primary Mission.

Primary scoring is capped at 45 VP, with no more than 15 VP per battle round.

### Fixed Secondary Missions

- Secretly choose Fixed play and two Fixed Missions, then reveal the choice.
- Display those cards face-up.
- They remain active and cannot be discarded.
- Secondary scoring is capped at 45 VP and 15 VP per battle round.
- Each Fixed Mission card can contribute no more than 20 VP.

### Tactical Secondary Missions

- Shuffle the Secondary Mission deck face-down.
- At the start of each Command phase, draw two active Secondary Missions face-up.
- Once per battle, at the end of the Command phase, spend 1CP to discard one active card and draw a replacement.
- At the end of each player's turn, score achieved missions and discard achieved Tactical cards.
- During the active player's own end-of-turn processing, they may discard any active Tactical missions to gain 1CP.

### Battle Ready and Victory

An army painted to Battle Ready standard scores 10 VP. The player with the most VP wins; equal scores produce a draw.

## Card Interpretation Notes

- A cumulative condition awards both its own VP and the normal condition's VP.
- Where alternatives are marked "or", only one listed condition scores.
- A unit leaves the battlefield if destroyed, embarked in a TRANSPORT, or removed by a rule.
- Underlined "one" means exactly one.
- VP above a printed limit is ignored.
- A "When Drawn" section applies only to Tactical play.

Version 1.0 also confirms that operation markers can only be removed when the Primary Mission permits it, and answers specific questions for Death Trap, Surveil the Foe, and Vital Link.

## Player Impact

Players must select the secondary mode secretly, track round and card caps, and distinguish Tactical-only instructions from persistent Fixed Missions.

## Organizer Impact

Organizers should ensure the current Chapter Approved FAQ is available and that score reporting preserves the printed caps. [[Team Events]] converts individual VP into additional match scores.
