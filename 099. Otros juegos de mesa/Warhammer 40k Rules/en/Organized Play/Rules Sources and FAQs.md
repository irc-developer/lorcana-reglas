# Rules Sources and FAQs

> This article describes tournament or organized play procedures. It does not replace the Warhammer 40,000 Core Rules.

## Summary

The event framework uses the Core Rules, the current Chapter Approved Mission Deck, and the Warhammer 40,000 app's Rules Appendix, faction errata, and FAQs.

## Scope

This article describes the rules resources named by the companions. Version 1.0 does not define judge powers, an appeal process, penalties, or a formal hierarchy for local rulings.

## Practical Procedure

1. Resolve the question from the Core Rules where possible.
2. For unusual or unclear interactions, consult the app's Rules Appendix.
3. Check applicable faction errata and FAQs.
4. Apply the Chapter Approved Mission Deck errata and FAQs printed in or issued for the event companion.

At version 1.0, the general companion lists no card errata but includes four Chapter Approved FAQ answers.

The companion does not state a further escalation or appeal procedure.

## Player Impact

Players should have access to current app resources and raise uncertainties before they affect score reporting where practical.

## Organizer Impact

Organizers should make the applicable documents and version cut-off clear. Any local judge or appeals procedure sits outside the published companion and should be identified separately.
