# Event Mission Sequence

> This article describes tournament or organized play procedures. It does not replace the Warhammer 40,000 Core Rules.

## Summary

The event mission sequence modifies how players prepare and resolve a Chapter Approved game in an organized event.

## Scope

This article covers the operational order of an event game. Layout selection, terrain placement, battlefield maps, and physical table measurements are intentionally excluded.

## Practical Procedure

1. Before the event, [[Muster Armies Step|muster the army]] in the Warhammer 40,000 app, select an available [[Force Dispositions|Force Disposition]], and record it on the roster.
2. Determine each player's Primary Mission from the opponent's Force Disposition symbol.
3. Determine Attacker and Defender by roll-off unless the specialist format says otherwise.
4. Secretly choose Fixed or Tactical Secondary Missions, then reveal the choices.
5. Secretly record embarked units and units starting in [[Strategic Reserves]], then reveal those decisions.
6. Alternate deploying units, beginning with the Defender. After setting up a TITANIC unit, skip the next opportunity to set up a unit.
7. Resolve redeployment rules after both armies deploy, alternating from the Attacker. Units put into Strategic Reserves at this step do not count toward the normal combined points cap.
8. Roll off for first turn, alternate pre-battle rules beginning with the first player, then begin the first battle round.
9. End the battle after five battle rounds. Turns continue to be played even if a player has no models remaining.
10. Determine the victor from total VP; a tie is a draw.

## Player Impact

Players must make secondary-mission and battle-formation decisions secretly before revealing them. They must also continue the battle to its defined end rather than ending automatically when one army has no models.

## Organizer Impact

The organizer must communicate any specialist-format changes and ensure players use the same sequence. Attacker/Defender handling differs in [[Team Events]].
