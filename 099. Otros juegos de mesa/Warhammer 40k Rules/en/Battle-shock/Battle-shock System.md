# Battle-shock System

## Overview

Battle-shock System is the authoritative navigation hub for the Battle-shock domain. This domain publishes the core battle-shock rule, the resulting state, the Command-phase application, and the neighbouring system links needed to understand how units become battle-shocked, how they recover, and which downstream systems consume that state.

## Scope

This domain covers the battle-shock roll as part of the canonical Battle-shock Rolls article, the battle-shocked state, the Command-phase application of battle-shock, and the immediate consequences that those rules impose on other systems.

It helps answer questions about when battle-shock is checked, what failing the roll does, how recovery happens, and which systems are disabled or modified while the state applies.

## Domain Ownership

- Owner domain: Battle-shock.
- Publication role: maintain the canonical explanation of battle-shock tests, battle-shocked state changes, Command-phase battle-shock timing, and the glossary support that makes those concepts navigable.
- Domain system role: provide the authoritative entry point and navigation surface for all published Battle-shock content.

## Domain Boundaries

Battle-shock owns:

- [[Battle-shock Rolls]] as the canonical rule that applies battle-shock.
- [[Command Phase Battle-shock]] as the timing surface that reapplies the roll and can remove the state.
- [[Battle-shocked]] as glossary support for the resulting unit state.

Battle-shock does not own:

- [[Objective Control System]] or objective ownership, even though battle-shock changes OC to -.
- [[Stratagem System]] or stratagem framework rules, even though battle-shock blocks some stratagem targeting.
- [[Actions]] or objective-side action procedures, even though battle-shock can stop actions from starting or completing.
- [[Core Concepts System]] concepts such as units, characteristics, or Leadership as a foundational roll family.
- [[Battle Sequence System]] timing-spine ownership of the Command phase itself.

## Related Systems

## Navigation

- Start with [[Battle-shock Rolls]] when the question is what the roll does, what failure applies, or why a unit became battle-shocked.
- Read [[Command Phase Battle-shock]] when the issue is when battle-shock is checked during the turn or how a unit stops being battle-shocked.
- Read [[Battle-shock Rolls]] when the term battle-shock roll itself needs clarification, because that alias resolves to the canonical rule article.
- Read [[Battle-shocked]] when the question is the state applied to a unit after failure.
- Move to [[Command Phase]] when the issue is the broader phase timing rather than the Battle-shock-owned application.
- Move to [[Objective Control System]] when the issue is how battle-shock changes participation in objective control rather than how battle-shock itself works.
- Move to [[Stratagem System]] when the issue is stratagem use, targeting, or Insane Bravery rather than the battle-shock-owned state change.
