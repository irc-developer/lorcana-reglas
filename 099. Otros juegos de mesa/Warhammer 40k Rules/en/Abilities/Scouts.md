# Scouts

## Summary

Scouts is the core ability that grants an eligible unit pre-battle scouting options before the battle begins.

## Definition

In the Resolve Pre-battle Abilities step, a unit where every model has Scouts X" may be set up wholly within its deployment zone from strategic reserves, make a scout move, or allow its DEDICATED TRANSPORT to scout if all embarked models also have Scouts.

## Rules

Every model in the unit must have Scouts X" for the unit to use the ability.

During the Resolve Pre-battle Abilities step, the unit can use one of its supported scouting options.

The ability can support a setup option from strategic reserves into the deployment zone, a [[Scout Move]], or a DEDICATED TRANSPORT scouting when all embarked models also have Scouts.

## Interactions

- [[Scout Move]] is the published movement procedure that one branch of this ability enables.

- [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] is the wider timing domain because this ability happens during Resolve Pre-battle Abilities before the battle begins.
- [[Core Abilities]] places Scouts inside the broader core-ability cluster.

## Restrictions

- The unit must have Scouts on every model.
- This article defines the ability, not the full movement procedure of [[Scout Move]].
- This article does not replace the wider pre-battle timing structure or deployment procedures owned by neighbouring domains.
