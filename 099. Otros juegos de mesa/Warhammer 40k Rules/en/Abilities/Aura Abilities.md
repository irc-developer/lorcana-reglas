# Aura Abilities

## Summary

Aura Abilities is the canonical article for the rule-type family whose effects apply to models or units within a stated range.

## Definition

Aura abilities affect models or units within a stated range.

A model is always within range of its own aura.

## Rules

An aura ability applies only within the range stated by that ability.

The source model is always within range of its own aura.

If multiple copies of the same aura could apply to a unit, that aura only applies once to that unit.

## Interactions

- [[Rule Types]] classifies aura abilities inside the wider ability taxonomy.
- [[Abilities]] distinguishes aura abilities from weapon abilities and other ability families.
- [[Abilities in Attached Units]] is a key downstream consumer when a unit-affecting aura is present inside an attached unit.
- [[Measuring Distances]] provides the baseline distance framework used when an aura checks whether a model or unit is within range.
- [[Keywords]] remains adjacent when an aura's conditions depend on faction or unit keywords.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the aura-ability family, not the full text of any one named aura.
- Multiple copies of the same aura do not stack on the same unit.
- This article does not redefine attached-unit propagation, keyword matching, or measurement rules owned by neighbouring domains.
