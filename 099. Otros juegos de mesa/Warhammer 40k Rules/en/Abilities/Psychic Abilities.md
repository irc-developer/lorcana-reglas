# Psychic Abilities

## Summary

Psychic Abilities is the canonical article for the rule-type family of abilities tagged Psychic.

## Definition

Psychic abilities are abilities tagged Psychic.

Wounds caused by a psychic ability are inflicted by a psychic attack.

## Rules

An ability tagged Psychic is a psychic ability.

If a psychic ability causes wounds, those wounds are inflicted by a psychic attack.

## Interactions

- [[Rule Types]] classifies psychic abilities inside the wider ability taxonomy.
- [[Abilities]] distinguishes psychic abilities from weapon abilities and other ability families.
- [[Psychic]] is the neighbouring weapon-side ability article for psychic-tagged weapons and should not be treated as the same concept as the psychic-ability family.
- [[Attack System]] is the downstream combat system that receives psychic attacks when a psychic ability causes wounds.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the psychic-ability family, not the full text of any one named psychic rule.
- This article does not replace the neighbouring [[Psychic]] weapon-ability article.
- This article does not redefine the attack procedure beyond the psychic-attack consequence stated by the core rules.
