# Lone Operative

## Summary

Lone Operative is the core ability that restricts enemy visibility and Indirect Fire targeting unless the attacking model is close enough, except while the unit is part of an attached unit.

## Definition

Lone Operative limits enemy visibility and Indirect Fire targeting beyond the ability's stated distance while the unit is not part of an attached unit.

## Rules

The default form prevents the unit from being visible to enemy models more than 12 inches away, unless the unit is part of an attached unit.

The unit also cannot be targeted by Indirect Fire weapons unless the attacking model is within 12 inches, unless the unit is part of an attached unit.

If the ability is written as Lone Operative X", use X inches instead of 12 inches.

## Interactions

- [[Visibility]] provides the baseline visibility rules.
- [[Indirect Fire]] is specifically restricted by this ability.
- [[Attached unit]] is the state that switches this protection off.

## Restrictions

- This article does not make the unit untargetable to nearby enemies.
- The ability is suppressed while the unit is part of an attached unit.
