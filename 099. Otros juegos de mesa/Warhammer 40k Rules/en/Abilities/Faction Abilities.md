# Faction Abilities

## Summary

Faction Abilities is the canonical article for the rule-type family shared by a faction and listed in the Faction Abilities section of a datasheet.

## Definition

Faction abilities are abilities common to a faction and listed in the Faction Abilities section of a datasheet.

## Rules

Faction abilities are presented in the Faction Abilities section of a datasheet.

They usually apply only if the selected army faction matches a faction keyword on the unit.

## Interactions

- [[Rule Types]] classifies faction abilities inside the wider ability taxonomy.
- [[Abilities]] distinguishes faction abilities from weapon abilities and other ability families.
- [[Datasheet System]] owns the unit-data structure that carries faction abilities without owning their semantics.
- [[Keywords]] is a direct prerequisite because faction abilities usually care about faction keywords on the unit.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the faction-ability family, not the text of any one faction rule.
- This article does not replace the datasheet structure that carries faction abilities.
- This article does not redefine keyword semantics owned by the Keywords domain.
