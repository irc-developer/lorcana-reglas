# Rule Types

## Summary

Rule Types is the ability-taxonomy concept that groups abilities into aura, faction, psychic, and wargear families.

## Definition

The Core Rules identify Aura Abilities, Faction Abilities, Psychic Abilities, and Wargear Abilities as distinct rule-type families.

## Rules

Aura Abilities affect models or units within a stated range.

A model is always within range of its own aura, and multiple copies of the same aura only apply once to a unit.

Faction Abilities are common to a faction and are listed in the Faction Abilities section of a datasheet.

Faction Abilities usually apply only if the selected army faction matches a faction keyword on the unit.

Psychic Abilities are abilities tagged Psychic, and wounds caused by a psychic ability are inflicted by a psychic attack.

Wargear Abilities are abilities granted by wargear.

If a Wargear Ability is tied to a unit-held item it applies to the unit, and if it is tied to a model-held item it applies until that bearer is destroyed.
