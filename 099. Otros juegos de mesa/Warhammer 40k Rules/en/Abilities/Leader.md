# Leader

## Summary

Leader is the core ability that allows a leader unit to form an attached unit with an eligible bodyguard unit before the battle.

## Definition

Leader is a core ability that points into the Attached Units rules for its procedure.

## Rules

Leader units can lead eligible bodyguard units as defined by the attached-unit rules and the unit-specific permissions provided by the official rules source.

The detailed procedure is owned by [[Forming Attached Units]].

## Interactions

- [[Attached Unit System]] owns the combined-unit rules.
- [[Forming Attached Units]] owns the procedure that uses Leader.

## Restrictions

- This article does not list every bodyguard unit a Leader can join.
