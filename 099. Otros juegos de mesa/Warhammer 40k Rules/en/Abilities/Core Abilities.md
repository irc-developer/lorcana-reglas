# Core Abilities

## Overview

Core Abilities is the authoritative System Article for the published core-ability and weapon-ability surfaces that currently matter most to attack declaration, attack resolution, shooting permissions, and attack-adjacent stratagem interactions.

## Scope

This page indexes the published ability-facing concepts that feed into the shared attack pipeline without redefining the attack steps themselves.

It helps readers move from the general ability taxonomy into the specific weapon-side modifiers and downstream consumer rules that alter how attacks are selected, resolved, or allocated.

## Domain Ownership

- Owner domain: Abilities.
- Publication role: provide the system-level index that routes readers from the general ability taxonomy into the published attack-facing core and weapon ability surfaces.
- Domain system role: provide the authoritative entry point and navigation surface for the Core Abilities cluster.

## Domain Boundaries

Abilities owns:

- [[Core Abilities]] as the navigation surface that indexes this cluster.

Abilities does not own:

- [[Weapon System]] ownership of individual weapon-side ability articles such as [[Anti]], [[Blast]], [[Precision]], or [[Torrent]].
- [[Attack System]] ownership of the attack steps that these abilities modify.
- [[Shooting System]] ownership of phase permissions such as [[Indirect Shooting]] or [[Close-quarters Shooting]].
- [[Attached Unit System]] ownership of attached-unit targeting and allocation rules even where abilities interact with them.
- [[Stratagem System]] ownership of stratagem procedures that temporarily grant or invoke attack-facing abilities.

## Related Systems

- [[Ability System]] is the broader taxonomy hub for ability families.
- [[Weapon System]] owns the weapon-profile context and the weapon-side meaning of individual weapon abilities.
- [[Attack System]] owns the attack declaration and hit-to-damage pipeline that these abilities modify.
- [[Shooting System]] owns the phase-level shooting permissions consumed by abilities such as [[Assault]], [[Close-quarters]], and [[Indirect Fire]].
- [[Attached Unit System]] owns the neighbouring allocation and targeting branch used by [[Precision]].
- [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] owns the wider timing structure that [[Scouts]] consumes before the battle begins.
- [[Stratagem System]] owns downstream stratagem procedures that can temporarily grant or invoke attack-facing abilities.

## Navigation

- Start here when the question is about which published ability concept modifies an attack rather than about the attack step itself.
- Move to [[Weapon System]] when the lookup is about weapon-profile ownership rather than ability routing.
- Move to [[Attack System]] when the lookup is about the shared attack procedure being modified.
- Read [[Scouts]] when the issue is a pre-battle core ability rather than an attack-facing weapon modifier.
- Read [[Assault]], [[Close-quarters]], or [[Indirect Fire]] when the issue is whether a weapon grants a special shooting mode.
- Read [[Anti]], [[Lethal Hits]], [[Sustained Hits]], [[Twin-linked]], [[Lance]], or [[Torrent]] when the issue is a specific hit or wound modifier.
- Read [[Blast]], [[Cleave]], [[Extra Attacks]], [[Rapid Fire]], or [[One Shot]] when the issue is attack gathering or weapon selection.
- Read [[Devastating Wounds]], [[Hazardous]], [[Melta]], [[Ignores Cover]], [[Precision]], or [[Psychic]] when the issue is a special damage, post-attack, defence, allocation, or modifier override.
- Move to [[Stratagem System]] when the ability interaction is being granted or invoked by a stratagem rather than by the weapon profile itself.
