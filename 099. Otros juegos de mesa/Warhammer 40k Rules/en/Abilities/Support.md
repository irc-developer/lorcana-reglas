# Support

## Summary

Support is the core ability that allows support units to join eligible bodyguard units through the Attached Units rules.

## Definition

Support is a core ability that points into the Attached Units rules for its procedure.

## Rules

Support units can be assigned to eligible bodyguard units as defined by the attached-unit rules and official unit permissions.

The detailed procedure is owned by [[Forming Attached Units]].

## Interactions

- [[Attached Unit System]] owns the combined-unit rules.
- [[Forming Attached Units]] owns the procedure that uses Support.

## Restrictions

- This article does not list every bodyguard unit a Support unit can join.
