# Super-heavy Walker

## Summary

Super-heavy Walker is the core ability that lets a unit move through many models and lower terrain sections, and optionally become MOBILE during a move at the risk of becoming battle-shocked.

## Definition

Super-heavy Walker modifies normal, advance, and fall-back moves for units that have the ability.

## Rules

Each time the unit makes a normal, advance, or fall-back move, models in the unit can move through models, including MONSTER and VEHICLE models, but excluding TITANIC models.

Those models can move horizontally through sections of terrain features that are 4 inches or less in height.

Before moving, the player can choose for all models in the unit to have the MOBILE keyword until that move ends.

If MOBILE is chosen this way, roll one D6 when the move ends; on a 1, the unit is battle-shocked.

## Interactions

- [[Movement System]] provides the move types modified by this ability.
- [[Terrain Categories]] and [[Moving Vertically]] provide neighbouring terrain movement rules.
- [[Battle-shock Rolls]] and [[Battle-shocked]] explain the resulting state when the risk roll fails.

## Restrictions

- This ability does not allow movement through TITANIC models.
- The optional MOBILE keyword lasts only until that move ends.
