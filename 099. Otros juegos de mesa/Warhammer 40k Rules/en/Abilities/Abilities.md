# Abilities

## Summary

Abilities is the umbrella rules concept that distinguishes core abilities from weapon abilities.

## Definition

Core abilities are listed on datasheets, and weapon abilities are listed in weapon profiles.

## Rules

Core abilities are carried by datasheets.

Weapon abilities are carried by weapon profiles.

Keyword-qualified weapon abilities only apply against targets with the named keyword.

Multiple instances of the same core ability or weapon ability are not cumulative.

If more than one instance could apply, the controlling player chooses which instance applies.

Numbered and keyworded variants still count as duplicates for this purpose.

## Restrictions

- This article distinguishes ability classes; it does not replace the specific rules of any individual ability.
- This article does not redefine datasheet structure, weapon-profile structure, or keyword semantics.
- This article does not absorb the rules of individual ability families such as aura or psychic abilities.
