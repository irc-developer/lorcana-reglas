# Feel No Pain

## Summary

Feel No Pain is the core ability that can prevent a model from losing a wound after it would otherwise lose one.

## Definition

Feel No Pain X+ is a numbered ability that rolls one D6 each time the model would lose a wound.

## Rules

This ability takes the form Feel No Pain X+.

Each time a model with this ability would lose a wound, roll one D6.

On X+, that wound is not lost.

## Interactions

- [[Inflict Damage]] and [[Mortal Wounds]] are common sources of wound loss that can lead into this prevention check.

## Restrictions

- This article does not change whether damage or mortal wounds were inflicted; it only covers whether the wound is ultimately lost.
