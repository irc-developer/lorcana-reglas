# Firing Deck

## Summary

Firing Deck is the core ability that lets a TRANSPORT temporarily use selected ranged weapons from models embarked within it when the TRANSPORT shoots.

## Definition

Firing Deck X lets a TRANSPORT selected to shoot borrow up to X eligible ranged weapons from embarked models for that shooting sequence.

## Rules

This ability takes the form Firing Deck X.

In your Shooting phase, when the TRANSPORT is selected to shoot and one or more units are embarked within it, select up to X embarked models whose units have not already been selected to shoot this phase.

For each selected model, select one of its ranged weapons, excluding One Shot weapons.

Until the TRANSPORT has resolved all of its attacks, it has those selected weapons in addition to its other weapons.

Until the end of the turn, units embarked within that TRANSPORT are not eligible to shoot.

## Interactions

- [[Transport System]] provides the embarked-unit context.
- [[Shooting Phase]] provides the timing window.
- [[Select Weapons]] is the attack step affected by the borrowed weapons.
- [[One Shot]] is excluded from the selected weapons.

## Restrictions

- Firing Deck belongs to the TRANSPORT shooting sequence; the embarked units themselves are not selected to shoot by this ability.
