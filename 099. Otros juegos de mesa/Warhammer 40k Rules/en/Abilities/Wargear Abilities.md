# Wargear Abilities

## Summary

Wargear Abilities is the canonical article for the rule-type family of abilities granted by wargear.

## Definition

Wargear abilities are abilities granted by wargear.

## Rules

If a wargear ability is tied to a unit-held item, it applies to the unit.

If a wargear ability is tied to a model-held item, it applies until that bearer is destroyed.

## Interactions

- [[Rule Types]] classifies wargear abilities inside the wider ability taxonomy.
- [[Abilities]] distinguishes wargear abilities from weapon abilities and other ability families.
- [[Wargear Options]] is the neighbouring datasheet-facing surface for wargear carried by the unit.
- [[Abilities in Attached Units]] is a key downstream consumer when a unit-affecting wargear ability is present inside an attached unit.
- [[Destroyed]] is a direct state dependency because model-held wargear abilities last until the bearer is destroyed.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the wargear-ability family, not the full text of any one named wargear rule.
- This article does not replace datasheet ownership of wargear options.
- This article does not redefine attached-unit propagation or destruction-state rules owned by neighbouring domains.
