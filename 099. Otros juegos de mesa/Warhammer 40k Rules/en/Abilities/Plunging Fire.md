# Plunging Fire

## Summary

Plunging Fire is the rule that improves the Ballistic Skill characteristic of some ranged attacks made from elevated terrain or by TOWERING models at nearby ground-level targets.

## Definition

Plunging Fire improves the BS characteristic of an attack by 1 when its height or TOWERING condition applies and the target unit is visible and contains one or more models on ground level.

## Rules

The target unit must be visible and contain one or more models on ground level.

The attack improves its BS characteristic by 1 if the attacking model is on a section of a terrain feature at least 3 inches high.

The attack also improves its BS characteristic by 1 if the attacking model has the TOWERING keyword and the target unit is within 12 inches.

## Restrictions

- Plunging Fire does not apply to attacks made by AIRCRAFT units or attacks targeting AIRCRAFT units.
- This article does not define terrain movement or visibility by itself.
