# Actions

## Summary

Actions is the rule for mission-defined or rule-defined battlefield tasks that use start timing, eligibility, completion timing, and effect to create objective-side mission interaction.

## Definition

Actions are mission or rule-defined battlefield tasks with a start timing, eligible units, use limit, completion timing, and effect.

## Rules

A unit cannot start an action if it is not on the battlefield, is AIRCRAFT or FORTIFICATION, is battle-shocked, has OC 0 or -, is engaged unless it is TITANIC, advanced or fell back that turn, or already started another action that turn.

After starting an action, until the end of the turn the unit cannot shoot, excluding TITANIC units, and cannot declare a charge.

If the unit makes a move other than a pile-in move or consolidation move, or if it leaves the battlefield, it does not complete the action.

## Interactions

- [[Battle-shock System]] can stop a unit from starting an action and can stop a started action from being completed, but Battle-shock remains a separate domain.
- [[Advance Move]] and [[Fall-back Move]] can make a unit ineligible to start an action that turn.
- [[Pile-in Move]] and [[Consolidation Move]] are the only listed moves that do not prevent action completion once the action has started.
- [[Movement System]] provides the move framework consumed by the completion restriction.
- [[Shooting System]] and [[Charge System]] remain owner of shooting and charge permissions even though this article blocks those activities after an action starts.

## Restrictions

- This article does not define the effect of a specific action; mission rules and other rules define those effects.
- A unit that fails any listed eligibility condition cannot start the action.
- A started action fails to complete if the unit leaves the battlefield or makes any move other than a pile-in move or consolidation move.
