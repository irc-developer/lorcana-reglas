# Aircraft Charging and Fighting

## Summary

Aircraft Charging and Fighting is the aircraft-specific rule for charge eligibility and melee targeting involving AIRCRAFT units.

## Definition

AIRCRAFT units cannot declare charges and have narrow melee targeting permissions.

## Rules

AIRCRAFT units are not eligible to declare a charge.

AIRCRAFT units can only make melee attacks that target FLYING units.

Only FLYING units can select AIRCRAFT units as charge targets.

Only FLYING models can make melee attacks that target AIRCRAFT units.

## Interactions

- [[Charge System]] owns the general charge framework.
- [[Fight System]] owns melee resolution.
- [[Flying Models]] identifies the units and models that can interact with AIRCRAFT in charge and melee contexts.

## Restrictions

- This article does not redefine the Charge Move or Fight procedure.
- AIRCRAFT restrictions apply specifically to AIRCRAFT units and the FLYING units or models interacting with them.
