# Aircraft Deployment

## Summary

Aircraft Deployment is the aircraft-specific setup rule that places all AIRCRAFT units into Strategic Reserves before the battle.

## Definition

During the Declare Battle Formations step, all AIRCRAFT units must be placed in Strategic Reserves.

## Rules

AIRCRAFT units are not deployed normally during this step.

Their later arrival uses the Strategic Reserves arrival framework unless another rule says otherwise.

## Interactions

- [[Aircraft]] is the parent specialist concept.
- [[Placing Units in Strategic Reserves]] provides the wider reserve-placement procedure.
- [[Strategic Reserves System]] provides the reserve-state framework.

## Restrictions

- This article does not define the full Declare Battle Formations step.
- This article does not itself make the aircraft arrive on the battlefield.
