# Aircraft Shooting

## Summary

Aircraft Shooting is the aircraft-specific shooting exception for Plunging Fire.

## Definition

The Plunging Fire rule has no effect on attacks made by AIRCRAFT units or attacks that target AIRCRAFT units.

## Rules

When an AIRCRAFT unit makes an attack, Plunging Fire does not modify that attack.

When an attack targets an AIRCRAFT unit, Plunging Fire does not modify that attack.

## Interactions

- [[Plunging Fire]] owns the general rule that this aircraft exception switches off.
- [[Shooting System]] owns the wider shooting procedure.

## Restrictions

- This article does not define AIRCRAFT target eligibility outside the stated Plunging Fire exception.
