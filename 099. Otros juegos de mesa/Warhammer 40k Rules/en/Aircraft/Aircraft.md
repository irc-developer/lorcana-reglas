# Aircraft

## Summary

Aircraft is the specialist concept for AIRCRAFT units, whose deployment, movement, shooting, charging, and fighting rules are heavily restricted.

## Definition

AIRCRAFT units are specialised units that use Strategic Reserves and aircraft-specific restrictions instead of the normal movement and engagement pattern.

## Rules

AIRCRAFT units must be placed in Strategic Reserves during the Declare Battle Formations step.

AIRCRAFT units are only eligible to make Ingress Moves.

AIRCRAFT units leave the battlefield again at the end of the opponent's turn.

AIRCRAFT units have special restrictions for shooting, charging, and melee targeting.

## Interactions

- [[Aircraft Deployment]] owns the reserve-placement requirement.
- [[Aircraft Movement]] owns the movement restrictions and aircraft interaction exceptions.
- [[Aircraft Shooting]] owns the Plunging Fire exception.
- [[Aircraft Charging and Fighting]] owns the charge and melee restrictions.
- [[Strategic Reserves System]] provides the reserve framework.

## Restrictions

- This article does not redefine Strategic Reserves or Ingress Move.
- AIRCRAFT is a keyword-facing specialist rule set, not a generic flying rule.
