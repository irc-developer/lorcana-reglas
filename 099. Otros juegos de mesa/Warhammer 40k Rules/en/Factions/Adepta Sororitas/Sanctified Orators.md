# Sanctified Orators

## Summary

Sanctified Orators is an Adepta Sororitas detachment focused on CHARACTER units, Leadership, and enhancement-style upgrades.

## Detachment Rule

The previewed detachment rule is Hymns of Battle.

Enhancements selected from this detachment do not count against the total number of enhancements in the army. Friendly ADEPTA SORORITAS CHARACTER units also improve their Leadership by 1.

## Enhancements

- Hagiomnifex is previewed as an Upgrade for ADEPTA SORORITAS CHARACTER units, excluding PENITENT units.
- Once per turn per unit, at the start of a phase, Hagiomnifex can choose from several effects: increase enemy [[Detection range]] while the unit shoots, automatically pass [[Battle-shock Rolls]], improve Move, improve attack Strength, or penalise Wound rolls made by attacks whose Strength is greater than the unit's Toughness.

## Stratagem Surface

The faction focus does not preview a specific Sanctified Orators stratagem. The official article notes that some new detachments use fewer dedicated bonuses and stratagems in exchange for unusually strong enhancements.

## Interactions

- [[Leadership Rolls]] and [[Battle-shock Rolls]] are the main rules hooks.
- [[Detection range]] matters for Rite of Revelation.
- [[Wound Rolls]] matter for Chorus of Repudiation.
- [[Enhancement Limit]] matters because this detachment's enhancements do not count against the normal total.
