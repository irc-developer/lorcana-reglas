# Adepta Sororitas

## Summary

Adepta Sororitas is the faction hub for the official Adepta Sororitas Faction Pack, version 1.1, and the current Imperial Faction Packs detachment table.

This wiki area records the faction-facing rules surfaces that are stable enough to navigate from the core rules: detachment selection, Detachment Points, Force Dispositions, detachment rules, faction-pack datasheets, and FAQ or errata notes.

## Scope

This hub covers:

- faction detachment navigation;
- the current Detachment Point and Force Disposition table;
- the three officially previewed new detachments: [[Chorus of Condemnation]], [[Sacred Champions]], and [[Sanctified Orators]];
- the [[Champions of Faith]] detachment from the faction pack PDF;
- faction-pack datasheet, FAQ, and errata navigation.

It does not replace the Codex, the Warhammer 40,000 app, points values, full datasheet cards, or the Munitorum Field Manual.

## Detachment Construction Notes

Adepta Sororitas detachments are selected through the general [[Detachment Points]] system. The faction article [[Adepta Sororitas Detachments]] records Detachment Point costs, Force Dispositions, confirmed restrictions, legal-combination examples, and source notes.

Force Disposition does not itself restrict detachment mixing. The confirmed specific restriction in the inspected sources is the REVEREND tag on [[Sacred Champions]], which prevents that detachment from being included with another REVEREND detachment.
