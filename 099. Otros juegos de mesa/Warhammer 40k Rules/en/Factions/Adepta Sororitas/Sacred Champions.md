# Sacred Champions

## Summary

Sacred Champions is an Adepta Sororitas detachment centred on Celestian Sacresants, improved attack characteristics, and mortal-wound defence.

## Detachment Rule

The previewed detachment rule is Holy Quest.

Attacks made by friendly CELESTIAN SACRESANTS units improve their Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill by 1.

## REVEREND Tag

This detachment has the REVEREND tag and cannot be included with another REVEREND detachment.

## Enhancements

- Writ of Compunction is previewed as an Upgrade for CELESTIAN SACRESANTS units. The unit adds 1 to its [[Objective Control (OC)]].

## Stratagem Surface

| Stratagem | Cost | Timing | Rules hook |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Faithful Fortitude | 1CP | Any phase, when a friendly CELESTIAN SACRESANTS unit suffers a mortal wound | Gives [[Feel No Pain]] 5+ against [[Mortal Wounds]] until the end of the phase. |

## Interactions

- [[Hit Rolls]] are indirectly affected by the WS/BS improvement.
- [[Objective Control (OC)]] is the Writ of Compunction hook.
- [[Mortal Wounds]] and [[Feel No Pain]] define Faithful Fortitude.
