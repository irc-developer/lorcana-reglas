# Chorus of Condemnation

## Summary

Chorus of Condemnation is an Adepta Sororitas detachment focused on INFANTRY FLY units, condemned enemy targets, and increasing enemy [[Detection range]].

## Detachment Rule

The previewed detachment rule is Angelic Judgement.

Friendly ADEPTA SORORITAS INFANTRY FLY units gain Condemnatory Psalms. In the Shooting phase, one such unit can select a visible enemy unit within 12"; that enemy unit is condemned.

While a unit is condemned, its [[Detection range]] is increased by 3".

## Enhancements

- Clarion of Urgency is previewed for a Jump Pack Canoness. At the end of the opponent's Fight phase, if the unit is not engaged, it can be placed into [[Strategic Reserves]].

## Stratagem Surface

| Stratagem | Cost | Timing | Rules hook |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Harmonised Exorcism | 1CP | Your Shooting phase, when a friendly Exorcist is selected to shoot | Selects a visible enemy unit near a friendly ADEPTA SORORITAS INFANTRY FLY unit; the Exorcist improves hit reliability against that target. |

## Interactions

- [[Hidden]] and [[Detection range]] are the detachment's main rules hooks.
- [[Deep Strike]] and [[Strategic Reserves]] matter for Jump Pack repositioning.
- [[Hit Rolls]] matter for Harmonised Exorcism.
