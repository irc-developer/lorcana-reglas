# Champions of Faith

## Summary

Champions of Faith is an Adepta Sororitas detachment from the official faction pack. Its rules engine selects Adepta Sororitas units as Righteous in the Command phase and rewards that state with movement, Leadership, attack, combat, and defensive benefits.

## Detachment Rule

The detachment rule is Righteous Purpose.

In the Command phase, up to three ADEPTA SORORITAS units from the army, including units embarked within [[Transports]], can become Righteous until the start of the next Command phase.

While a unit is Righteous:

- models in the unit add 1" to their Move characteristic;
- models in the unit improve Leadership by 1;
- weapons equipped by Battle Sisters Squad, Celestian Sacresants, and Paragon Warsuits models in the unit improve Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill by 1.

## Keywords And Additional Rule

While a Celestian Sacresants unit from the army is not [[Battle-shocked]], Celestian Sacresants models in that unit add 1 to [[Objective Control (OC)]].

## Enhancements

- Triptych of Judgement lets the bearer ignore modifiers to the unit's attack WS/BS and Hit rolls.
- Mark of Devotion improves the bearer's melee attacks; the bonus improves while the unit is Righteous.
- Eyes of the Oracle gives the bearer's weapons [[Precision]] and generates 1CP when the unit destroys an enemy CHARACTER model.
- Sanctified Amulet blocks enemy units arriving from Reserves from being set up within 12" of the bearer.

## Interactions

- [[Leadership Rolls]], movement, WS/BS, and [[Hit Rolls]] are the basic Righteous Purpose hooks.
- [[Precision]], [[Lethal Hits]], [[Sustained Hits]], [[Feel No Pain]], and [[Mortal Wounds]] define several enhancements and stratagems.
- [[Strategic Reserves]], [[Deep Strike]], and set-up restrictions matter for Sanctified Amulet.
- [[Fall-back Move]], [[Pile-in Move]], and [[Consolidation Move]] appear in the mobility and combat stratagems.
