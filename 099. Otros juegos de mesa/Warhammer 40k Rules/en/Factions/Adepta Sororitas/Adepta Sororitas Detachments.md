# Adepta Sororitas Detachments

## Summary

Adepta Sororitas Detachments is the navigation surface for the faction's current Detachment Point and Force Disposition table.

The current inspected sources include full or previewed rules for [[Chorus of Condemnation]], [[Sacred Champions]], [[Sanctified Orators]], and [[Champions of Faith]]. The remaining Codex detachments are listed here where the current official Imperial table gives their Detachment Point cost and Force Disposition.

## Detachments With Wiki Articles

| Detachment | Primary focus | Main rules hook |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[Chorus of Condemnation]] | Seraphim, Zephyrim, and INFANTRY FLY units | Condemned enemy units, [[Detection range]], Exorcist support, and Jump Pack Canoness repositioning. |
| [[Sacred Champions]] | Celestian Sacresants | Weapon Skill and Ballistic Skill boosts, REVEREND tag, Objective Control, and mortal-wound defence. |
| [[Sanctified Orators]] | Adepta Sororitas CHARACTER units | Leadership, enhancements that do not count against the army limit, and Hagiomnifex unit upgrades. |
| [[Champions of Faith]] | Righteous Adepta Sororitas units | Move, Leadership, WS/BS, Celestian Sacresants, melee pressure, [[Precision]], and fallback activity. |

## Mixing Adepta Sororitas Detachments

Adepta Sororitas detachments can be mixed if the combined Detachment Point cost fits the battle size and all detachment-specific restrictions are satisfied.

At Strike Force size, with 3DP available, examples include:

| Combination | Legal? | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [[Chorus of Condemnation]] + [[Sacred Champions]] + [[Sanctified Orators]] | Yes, unless another unpublished restriction applies | 1DP + 1DP + 1DP; unlocks Reconnaissance, Take and Hold, and Purge the Foe. |
| [[Chorus of Condemnation]] + Army of Faith | Yes | 1DP + 2DP. |
| [[Sacred Champions]] + Penitent Host | Requires tag check | 1DP + 2DP; [[Sacred Champions]] cannot be taken with another REVEREND detachment. |
| Bringers of Flame | Yes | 3DP; uses all Strike Force DP. |
| Hallowed Martyrs + [[Sanctified Orators]] | No at Strike Force | 3DP + 1DP exceeds 3DP. |

## Confirmed Restrictions

- [[Sacred Champions]] has the REVEREND tag.
- It cannot be included with another REVEREND detachment.
- No equivalent tag has been confirmed for the other inspected Adepta Sororitas detachments.

## Mission Selection

If an Adepta Sororitas army unlocks multiple [[Force Dispositions]], one available Force Disposition is chosen to represent the army for the battle. That chosen Force Disposition, not the highest-cost detachment and not the number of detachments in each category, is what is used for mission context.
