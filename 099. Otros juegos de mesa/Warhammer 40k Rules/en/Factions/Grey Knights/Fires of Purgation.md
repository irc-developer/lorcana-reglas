# Fires of Purgation

## Summary

Fires of Purgation is a Grey Knights detachment built around Purgation Squads applying ranged pressure and punishing battle-shocked enemies.

It connects Purgation Squad targeting with battle-shock, improves their special shooting profile, and gives them stratagems for falling back and shooting, spiking ranged attacks, and converting hits into mortal wounds against battle-shocked targets.

## Detachment Rule

Searing Soulflame adds battle-shock pressure when a Purgation Squad uses its Righteous Persecution ability to pin an enemy unit.

The targeted enemy unit makes the relevant battle-shock roll with a penalty.

## Enhancements

- Precognicient Volleys improves Purgation Squad snap shooting by letting those attacks hit on a better unmodified hit-roll threshold.
- Boons of Deimos improves the Strength of Purgation Squad ranged attacks.

## Stratagem Surfaces

| Stratagem | Cost | Timing surface | Rules hook |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Soul-locked | 1CP | Movement phase, when a Purgation Squad is selected to make a fall-back move | The fall-back move does not stop the unit from being eligible to shoot. |
| Focused Immolation | 1CP | Shooting phase, when a Purgation Squad is selected to shoot | Ranged attacks into one selected enemy unit gain [[Devastating Wounds]] and [[Sustained Hits]]. |
| Spiritsear | 1CP | Shooting phase, after a Purgation Squad has shot | A battle-shocked enemy hit by those attacks suffers mortal wounds. |

## Interactions

- [[Battle-shock Rolls]] and [[Battle-shocked]] are the detachment's pressure axis.
- [[Snap Shooting]] defines the special shooting surface modified by Precognicient Volleys.
- [[Fall-back Move]] normally limits shooting; Soul-locked overrides that consequence for the target unit.
- [[Devastating Wounds]], [[Sustained Hits]], and [[Mortal Wounds]] are the main damage hooks.
