# Immaterial Interdiction

## Summary

Immaterial Interdiction is a Grey Knights detachment centred on Interceptor Squad mobility.

It lets Interceptors convert shooting-phase activity into extra movement, supports early ingress, adds stealth protection, and gives stratagems for charge impact, action economy, and reactive movement after enemies fall back.

## Detachment Rule

Echojump can be used in the Grey Knights Shooting phase after a friendly Interceptor Squad has shot, provided that unit did not make an ingress move that turn.

If used, the unit gives up Personal Teleporters for that turn and instead makes a short surge move.

## Enhancements

- Predestined Coordinates allows an Interceptor Squad to make an ingress move in the first Grey Knights Movement phase.
- Astral Overlap gives an Interceptor Squad [[Stealth]].

## Stratagem Surfaces

| Stratagem | Cost | Timing surface | Rules hook |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Blades from the Beyond | 1CP | Fight phase, when a charging Interceptor Squad is selected to fight | Melee attacks gain [[Lance]]. |
| By Thought Alone | 1CP | Shooting phase, when an Interceptor Squad starts an action | The action does not stop the unit from being eligible to shoot. |
| Responsive Displacement | 1CP | Opponent's Movement phase, after an enemy that was engaged with an Interceptor Squad ends a fall-back move and the Interceptor Squad is unengaged | The Interceptor Squad can make a short normal move. |

## Interactions

- [[Shooting Phase]], [[Surge Move]], [[Ingress Move]], and [[Normal Move]] define the movement timing.
- [[Actions]] matters because By Thought Alone changes the normal action-and-shoot tension.
- [[Lance]] rewards successful charges.
- [[Fall-back Move]] is the trigger surface for Responsive Displacement.

## Restrictions

Echojump is unavailable if the same Interceptor Squad made an ingress move that turn.
