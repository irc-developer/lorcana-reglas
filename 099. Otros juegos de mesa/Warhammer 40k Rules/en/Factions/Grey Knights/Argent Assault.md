# Argent Assault

## Summary

Argent Assault is a Grey Knights detachment focused on Paladin Squads fighting elite or tougher targets and surviving mortal wounds.

Its rules package rewards sending Paladins into hard melee matchups and gives them tools to charge, mark hidden enemies, protect against mortal wounds, pick out key models, and punish enemy melee attackers.

## Detachment Rule

Dauntless Champions improves Paladin melee reliability when their attacks are weaker than the target's Toughness.

When the condition is met, the Paladin Squad gains a bonus to wound rolls for those attacks.

## Enhancements

- Psychic Celerity supports Terminator-model charges.
- Vigilance of Titan lets a Terminator-model unit increase an enemy unit's detection range from the start of the Grey Knights Shooting phase.

## Stratagem Surfaces

| A Threat Ended | 1CP | Fight phase, when a Paladin Squad is selected to fight | Gives melee attacks [[Precision]]. |
| Aura of Vengeance | 1CP | Fight phase, when an enemy targets a Paladin Squad | Makes that enemy unit's melee attacks [[Hazardous]]. |

## Interactions

- [[Wound Rolls]] are central because the detachment rule modifies wound reliability.
- [[Mortal Wounds]] and [[Feel No Pain]] define Truesilver Aegis.
- [[Precision]] lets melee attacks threaten attached Characters through the normal attack-allocation framework.
- [[Hazardous]] creates risk for enemy melee attackers.
- [[Detection range]] matters for Vigilance of Titan.
