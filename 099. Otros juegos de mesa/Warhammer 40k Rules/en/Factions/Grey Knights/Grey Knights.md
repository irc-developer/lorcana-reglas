# Grey Knights

## Summary

Grey Knights is the faction hub for the Grey Knights Faction Pack, version 1.0.

The pack adds faction-specific detachments, faction rule updates, and additional datasheet material. This wiki area records the rules-facing concepts that are stable enough to navigate from the core rules without turning datasheets into full copied unit cards.

## Scope

This hub covers:

- faction detachment navigation;
- the four faction-pack detachments;
- Grey Knights Detachment Point and Force Disposition navigation;
- the Hallowed Ground faction concept;
- faction rules updates that affect existing Grey Knights rules and datasheets.

It does not attempt to replace the Warhammer 40,000 app, points values, datasheet cards, or Codex ownership.

## Detachment Construction Notes

Grey Knights detachments are selected through the general [[Detachment Points]] system. The faction article [[Grey Knights Detachments]] records the known Grey Knights Detachment Point costs, Force Dispositions, legal combination patterns, and mission-selection notes.

Force Disposition does not restrict which Grey Knights detachments can be mixed. It determines which mission-facing category the army can choose to represent itself with before a battle.
