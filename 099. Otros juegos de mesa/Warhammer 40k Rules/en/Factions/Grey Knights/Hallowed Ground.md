# Hallowed Ground

## Summary

Hallowed Ground is the Warpbane Task Force detachment rule that marks parts of the battlefield as consecrated areas for a Grey Knights army.

It acts as a spatial condition: some areas are always Hallowed Ground, some become Hallowed Ground because of Purifier Squads, and some become Hallowed Ground temporarily through objective control.

## Definition

Hallowed Ground is the area of the battlefield treated as being within a Warpbane Task Force Grey Knights army's consecrated ground.

A unit can be affected either because it is a Purifier Squad or because it is wholly within Hallowed Ground, depending on the specific rule being resolved.

## Areas Included

An army's Hallowed Ground includes:

- that army's own deployment zone;
- the battlefield area within range of one or more friendly Purifier Squads;
- No Man's Land for a phase, if the Grey Knights player controls at least half of the objective markers there at the start of that phase;
- the opponent's deployment zone for a phase, if the Grey Knights player controls at least half of the objective markers there at the start of that phase.

## Attack Effect

Grey Knights units using the Warpbane Task Force detachment get a basic hit-roll re-roll benefit when attacking a visible ranged target or making melee attacks.

If the attacking unit is a Purifier Squad and/or is wholly within Hallowed Ground, the hit re-roll benefit improves.

## Interactions

- [[Deployment zone]] determines two of the possible battlefield areas.
- [[Objective marker]] and [[Objective Control System]] matter when checking No Man's Land or the opponent's deployment zone.
- [[Hit Rolls]] define the attack benefit and its re-roll window.
- [[Warpbane Task Force]] contains the enhancements and stratagems that repeatedly check whether a unit is wholly within Hallowed Ground.

## Restrictions

- The No Man's Land and opponent-deployment-zone portions are checked at the start of a phase and last only until the end of that phase.
- Some effects require the unit to be wholly within Hallowed Ground, not merely partly within it.
