# Warpbane Task Force

## Summary

Warpbane Task Force is a Grey Knights detachment built around [[Hallowed Ground]], Purifier Squads, and control of battlefield regions.

The detachment turns deployment zones, Purifier proximity, and objective control into a spatial rules engine that improves Grey Knights attacks and powers several defensive, positional, and mortal-wound stratagems.

## Detachment Rule

The detachment rule is [[Hallowed Ground]].

Use the dedicated article for the precise concept. In practice, the detachment asks whether a Grey Knights unit is a Purifier Squad and/or wholly within the army's Hallowed Ground, then improves hit re-roll access accordingly.

## Enhancements

- Mandulian Reliquary increases Objective Control for the bearer while the bearer’s unit is not battle-shocked.
- Radiant Champion gives the bearer [[Precision]] in melee and adds mortal-wound pressure while the bearer is wholly within Hallowed Ground.
- Phial of the Abyss gives the bearer’s unit [[Stealth]].
- Paragon of Sanctity can temporarily treat one visible friendly Grey Knights unit as being within Hallowed Ground.

## Stratagem Surfaces

| Stratagem | Cost | Timing surface | Rules hook |
| --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Sanctified Kill Zone | 1CP | Grey Knights Shooting phase or Fight phase | A Grey Knights unit wholly within Hallowed Ground improves wound re-rolls; Purifier Squads benefit more. |
| Flames of Sanctity | 1CP | End of Fight phase | A Purifier Squad can deal mortal wounds to nearby enemy units, improved by Castellan Crowe. |
| Hallowed Beacon | 1CP | Reinforcements step of the Grey Knights Movement phase | A non-Terminator Grey Knights Infantry unit arriving by Deep Strike can be set up inside Hallowed Ground and closer than normal. |
| Fires of Covenant | 1CP | Start of opponent's Movement phase | A Grey Knights Infantry unit threatens mortal wounds when enemies are set up or end movement nearby, with a bonus if wholly within Hallowed Ground. |
| Aegis Eternal | 1CP | Opponent's Shooting phase, after targets are selected | Grey Knights Infantry models wholly within Hallowed Ground gain an invulnerable save. |
| Repelling Sphere | 1CP | Start of opponent's Charge phase | Enemy charge rolls are reduced against the target unit, with a stronger penalty if the target is wholly within Hallowed Ground. |

## Interactions

- [[Hallowed Ground]] is the core rule engine.
- [[Objective Control (OC)]], [[Terrain objective]], and [[Deployment zone]] determine which areas may be included.
- [[Deep Strike]] and setup restrictions matter for Hallowed Beacon.
- [[Mortal Wounds]], invulnerable saves, and [[Charge Move]] define several stratagem consequences.
