# Strategic Reserves

## Summary

Strategic Reserves is the off-battlefield reserve state used to keep eligible units out of play until they later enter the battlefield by making an [[Ingress Move]].

## Definition

Strategic Reserves are units placed off the battlefield to arrive later by ingress move.

## Rules

### Placing Units in Strategic Reserves

During the Declare Battle Formations Step, any friendly units except FORTIFICATIONS can be placed in Strategic Reserves.

The combined points value of units placed in Strategic Reserves, including units embarked within TRANSPORTS that are also placed in Strategic Reserves, cannot exceed 50% of the battle size points limit unless another rule states otherwise.

The current source does not define the full Placing Units in Strategic Reserves procedure, so this section records only the explicit permissions and limits stated here.

### Arriving from Strategic Reserves

Units in Strategic Reserves enter the battlefield by making an [[Ingress Move]], normally from the second [[Battle Round]] onwards.

At the end of the third [[Battle Round]], undeployed units still in Strategic Reserves are destroyed unless they are embarked within a TRANSPORT that made an [[Ingress Move]] during the battle or they are [[Repositioned Units]].

## Interactions

- [[Battle Round]] provides the numbered round timing that controls default arrival and destruction deadlines for Strategic Reserves.
- [[Ingress Move]] defines how a unit is set up when it arrives from Strategic Reserves.
- [[Repositioned Units]] use the same reserve state during the battle but are not the same as units that started the battle in [[Strategic Reserves]].
- Rapid Ingress changes the timing of an otherwise valid [[Ingress Move]].
- Deep Strike overrides the normal battlefield-edge arrival restriction of an [[Ingress Move]].
- [[Scouts]] can move some eligible units from [[Strategic Reserves]] into a [[Deployment zone]] during Resolve Pre-battle Abilities.

## Restrictions

- FORTIFICATIONS cannot be placed in [[Strategic Reserves]].
- The default points cap for units placed in [[Strategic Reserves]] is 50% of the battle size points limit.
- Unless another rule states otherwise, units in [[Strategic Reserves]] cannot arrive before the second [[Battle Round]].
- Unless an explicit exception applies, undeployed units in [[Strategic Reserves]] are destroyed at the end of the third [[Battle Round]].
