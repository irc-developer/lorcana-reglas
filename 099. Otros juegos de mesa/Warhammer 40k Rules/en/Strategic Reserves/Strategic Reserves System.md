# Strategic Reserves System

## Overview

Strategic Reserves System maps how reserve placement, reserve state, arrival, and reserve-specific overrides relate to each other.

## Scope

This domain covers reserve placement, reserve state, reserve arrival, reserve-specific movement, and the glossary support currently published around those mechanics.

It helps answer questions about how units enter Strategic Reserves, how they return to the battlefield, which related concepts remain in this domain, and when a reader should move into pre-battle, movement, aircraft, or keyword systems.

## Domain Ownership

- Owner domain: Strategic Reserves.
- Publication role: maintain the reserve-state, reserve-arrival, and reserve-specific movement surfaces that belong to this domain.
- Domain system role: provide the authoritative entry point and navigation surface for published and planned Strategic Reserves content.

## Domain Boundaries

Strategic Reserves owns:

- [[Strategic Reserves]], [[Ingress Move]], and [[Repositioned Units]] as the published reserve-domain articles.
- [[Deployment zone]] as glossary support currently used by this domain.
- the planned reserve surfaces Arriving from Strategic Reserves and Placing Units in Strategic Reserves.

Strategic Reserves does not own:

- [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] ownership of army setup and declaration steps that happen before reserve state is entered.
- [[Movement System]] ownership of generic movement procedures outside reserve arrival.
- [[Keyword System]] ownership of keyword semantics used by reserve-facing rules.
- [[Aircraft System]] ownership of aircraft-specific rules that consume reserve handling without becoming reserve concepts.

## Navigation

- Start here when the question is about reserve placement, reserve state, or returning to the battlefield from reserves.
- Read [[Strategic Reserves]] first for the canonical reserve-state surface.
- Read [[Ingress Move]] when the issue is how a unit enters the battlefield from reserves.
- Read [[Repositioned Units]] when another rule removes a unit from the battlefield and returns it through a reserve loop.
- Read [[Deployment zone]] when the question depends on the current glossary support used by this domain.
- Move to [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] when the issue is primarily about declaration or army setup before reserve handling begins.
- Move to [[Movement System]] when the lookup becomes about generic movement rather than reserve arrival.
- Move to [[Aircraft System]] when the question is specifically about aircraft as reserve consumers.
