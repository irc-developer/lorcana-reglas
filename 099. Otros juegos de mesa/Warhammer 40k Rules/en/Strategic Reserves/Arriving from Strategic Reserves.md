# Arriving from Strategic Reserves

## Summary

Arriving from Strategic Reserves is the reserve-arrival procedure that requires Strategic Reserves units to return to the battlefield by making an Ingress Move.

## Definition

A Strategic Reserves unit arrives on the battlefield by making an [[Ingress Move]].

## Rules

Unless another rule says otherwise, Strategic Reserves units can only arrive from the second battle round onwards.

At the end of the third battle round, Strategic Reserves units that have not made one or more Ingress Moves are destroyed unless an exception applies.

The default destruction rule does not apply to units embarked within TRANSPORTS that have made an Ingress Move during the battle.

The default destruction rule does not apply to [[Repositioned Units]].

## Interactions

- [[Ingress Move]] owns the set-up distance and battlefield-edge restrictions for the arrival move.
- [[Strategic Reserves]] owns the reserve state being exited.
- [[Repositioned Units]] explains the during-battle exception.
- [[Transport System]] is relevant when transported units are protected by a transport that has already arrived.

## Restrictions

- This article does not redefine the Ingress Move itself.
- This article does not override a source rule that gives a unit a different arrival permission or timing.
