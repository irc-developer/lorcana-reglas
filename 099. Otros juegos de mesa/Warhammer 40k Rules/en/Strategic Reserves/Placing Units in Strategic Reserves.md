# Placing Units in Strategic Reserves

## Summary

Placing Units in Strategic Reserves is the before-battle procedure that puts selected friendly units aside instead of deploying them on the battlefield.

## Definition

Before the battle, during the Declare Battle Formations step, eligible friendly units can be placed in Strategic Reserves instead of being deployed.

## Rules

One or more friendly units can be selected, excluding FORTIFICATIONS.

Units placed this way are not set up on the battlefield during deployment; they are kept aside as Strategic Reserves units.

Unless another rule says otherwise, the combined points value of Strategic Reserves units cannot exceed 50% of the points limit for the battle size.

That points limit includes units embarked within TRANSPORTS that are themselves placed in Strategic Reserves.

## Interactions

- [[Strategic Reserves]] defines the reserve state entered by the selected units.
- [[Arriving from Strategic Reserves]] governs their later arrival.
- [[Transport System]] is relevant when embarked units are counted as part of a transport placed in reserves.
- [[Aircraft System]] consumes this placement rule because AIRCRAFT units must be placed in Strategic Reserves.

## Restrictions

- This procedure does not itself set up the unit on the battlefield.
- This article does not define mission deployment or army construction beyond reserve placement.
