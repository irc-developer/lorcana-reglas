# Attached Unit System

## Overview

Attached Unit System is the primary entry point for the attached-unit domain. It maps the parent article, the attached-unit child articles, the adjacent interaction surfaces, and the recommended reading path for this rule family.

## Scope

This domain covers how attached units are formed and how that combined-unit state affects attacks, keywords, abilities, and glossary-level terminology inside the attached-unit branch.

It helps answer questions about the structure of an attached unit, which interactions belong to the attached-unit subtree, and when a reader should move into generic keyword, ability, attack, or core-concept systems instead of staying in attached-unit-specific handling.

## Domain Ownership

- Owner domain: Attached Units.
- Publication role: maintain the canonical attached-unit article family, including formation, attack-side interactions, keyword handling, ability handling, and glossary support for the combined-unit state.
- Domain system role: provide the authoritative entry point and navigation surface for published Attached Units content.

## Domain Boundaries

Attached Units owns:

- [[Attached unit]] as glossary support for the combined-unit state.
- [[Forming Attached Units]], [[Attacking Attached Units]], [[Keywords in Attached Units]], and [[Abilities in Attached Units]] as the published attached-unit article family.

Attached Units does not own:

- Leader and Support as datasheet-facing ability entry points outside this domain's published article family.
- Precision as an attack-side interaction surface owned outside the attached-unit subtree.
- [[Mixed Keywords in Units]] as the generic mixed-keyword article outside attached-unit-specific handling.
- [[Revived]] as a general state-transition surface that can reconnect to an attached unit without becoming an attached-unit-owned rule.

## Navigation

- Start here when the question is about the structure of an attached unit rather than a single downstream interaction.
- Read [[Attached unit]] first for the glossary surface that anchors the combined-unit concept.
- Read [[Forming Attached Units]] first among the rule articles when the issue is how the combined unit is created.
- Move to [[Attacking Attached Units]] when the lookup is about attack targeting, allocation, or saving against an attached unit.
- Move to [[Keywords in Attached Units]] when the lookup is about keyword behaviour inside the combined unit.
- Move to [[Abilities in Attached Units]] when the lookup is about ability propagation or interaction inside the combined unit.
- Move to [[Keyword System]] and [[Mixed Keywords in Units]] when the question becomes about generic keyword aggregation outside the attached-unit branch.
- Move to [[Attack System]] when the lookup is really about the shared attack pipeline rather than the attached-unit-specific modification of it.
- Move to [[Core Concepts System]] and [[Revived]] when the lookup becomes about baseline model state rather than attached-unit structure.
