# Attached unit

## Summary

Attached unit is the single unit formed when eligible leader and support units join a bodyguard unit through [[Forming Attached Units]].

## Definition

Single unit formed from a bodyguard unit plus eligible leader and support components, treated as one unit for all rules purposes.
