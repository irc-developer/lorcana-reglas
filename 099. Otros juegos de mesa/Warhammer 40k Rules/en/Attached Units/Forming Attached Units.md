# Forming Attached Units

## Summary

[[Forming Attached Units]] is the before-the-battle procedure that allows eligible leader and support units to join a bodyguard unit and become a single [[Attached unit]] for all rules purposes.

## Definition

Leader and support units can join bodyguard units before the battle to form attached units. An attached unit is a single unit for all rules purposes.

## Rules

[[Forming Attached Units]] happens before the battle when eligible leader and support units are assigned to a bodyguard unit.

Leader and support units can only lead specific bodyguard units listed externally.

Unless otherwise stated, each bodyguard unit can have only one leader unit and one support unit attached.

## Interactions

- [[Attached Unit System]] is the domain map for this rule family.
- Leader and Support are datasheet-facing abilities that rely on Forming Attached Units.
- [[Attacking Attached Units]] defines how an attached unit is attacked after it has been formed.
- [[Keywords in Attached Units]] defines how keywords behave once the attached unit exists.
- [[Abilities in Attached Units]] defines how unit-affecting abilities propagate inside the attached unit.

## Restrictions

- A leader or support unit can only join a bodyguard unit that it is specifically allowed to lead.
- Unless another rule states otherwise, a bodyguard unit can have no more than one leader unit attached.
- Unless another rule states otherwise, a bodyguard unit can have no more than one support unit attached.
