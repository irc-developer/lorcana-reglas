# Abilities in Attached Units

## Summary

Abilities in Attached Units is the rule that determines how unit-affecting abilities from one component of an [[Attached unit]] apply across that attached unit while the relevant source persists.

## Definition

Unit-affecting abilities from any component of an [[Attached unit]] apply to every model in that attached unit while the relevant source persists.

## Rules

Unit-affecting abilities from any component of an [[Attached unit]] apply to every model in that attached unit while the relevant source persists.

Model-specific effects apply only to that model.

Effects sourced from a leader or support component last until the last model in that leader or support unit is destroyed.

Effects sourced from the bodyguard component last until the last model in that bodyguard unit is destroyed.

Effects sourced from a specific model last until that model is destroyed.

If the source model is destroyed by an attack, the conferred effect lasts until the attacking unit finishes resolving all of its attacks.

## Interactions

- [[Forming Attached Units]] defines the structure that allows these unit-affecting abilities to propagate.
- [[Keywords in Attached Units]] governs keyword inheritance separately from ability propagation.
- [[Aura Abilities]] is a related ability family that can continue to matter while the relevant source persists.
- [[Revived]] is related when restored models interact with ongoing attached-unit state.
- [[Attached Unit System]] maps this article as the ability-propagation branch of the domain.

## Restrictions

- Model-specific effects do not spread across the whole [[Attached unit]].
- The duration of a propagated effect depends on whether its source is a bodyguard unit, a leader or support unit, or a specific model.
