# Psychic

## Summary

Psychic is the weapon ability that allows attacks to ignore any or all modifiers to BS or WS and any or all modifiers to the hit roll, and marks them as psychic attacks.

## Definition

Attacks made with Psychic weapons can ignore any or all modifiers to BS or WS and any or all modifiers to the hit roll. They are psychic attacks.

## Rules

Psychic modifies how attacks interact with BS, WS, and hit-roll modifiers.

Attacks made with Psychic weapons are psychic attacks.
