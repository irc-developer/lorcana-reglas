# Sustained Hits

## Summary

Sustained Hits is the weapon ability that causes a critical hit to score additional hits.

## Definition

On a critical hit, the attack scores X additional hits.

## Rules

Sustained Hits triggers on a critical hit during [[Hit Rolls]].

When triggered, the attack scores the listed additional hits.

## Interactions

- [[Hit Rolls]] is the upstream step that can produce the critical hit.
- [[Attack System]] is the shared attack pipeline that this ability modifies.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Sustained Hits inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- [[Core Abilities]] provides the wider attack-facing ability index.

## Restrictions

- Sustained Hits applies only on a critical hit.
- Sustained Hits adds hits; it does not itself resolve wound rolls or damage.
