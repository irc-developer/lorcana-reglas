# Lethal Hits

## Summary

Lethal Hits is the weapon ability that lets a critical hit automatically wound the target instead of making a wound roll.

## Definition

On a critical hit, you can choose for the attack to automatically wound the target.

## Rules

Lethal Hits triggers on a critical hit during [[Hit Rolls]].

If you use it, no wound roll is made for that attack.

Because no wound roll is made, the attack cannot become a critical wound and cannot trigger rules that require a critical wound.

## Interactions

- [[Hit Rolls]] is the upstream step that can produce the critical hit.
- [[Devastating Wounds]] is a neighbouring rule that requires a critical wound and therefore is not triggered if no wound roll is made.
- [[Attack System]] is the shared pipeline that this ability modifies.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Lethal Hits inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.

## Restrictions

- Lethal Hits applies only on a critical hit.
- If you use Lethal Hits, no wound roll is made.
- An attack that skips the wound roll cannot become a critical wound.
