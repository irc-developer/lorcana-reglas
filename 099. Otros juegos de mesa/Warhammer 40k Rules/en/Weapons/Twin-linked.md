# Twin-linked

## Summary

Twin-linked is the weapon ability that allows the wound roll to be re-rolled.

## Definition

You can re-roll the wound roll.

## Rules

Twin-linked modifies [[Wound Rolls]].

It allows the wound roll to be re-rolled.

## Interactions

- [[Wound Rolls]] is the attack-sequence step that Twin-linked modifies.
- [[Attack System]] is the shared attack pipeline that receives the re-rolled result.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Twin-linked inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- Command Re-roll is a related re-roll rule referenced by the source.

## Restrictions

- Twin-linked changes wound-roll handling only.
- This article does not redefine the general wound-roll procedure.
