# Torrent

## Summary

Torrent is the weapon ability that causes attacks to hit automatically.

## Definition

Attacks automatically hit.

## Rules

Torrent modifies the normal hit-determination handling in [[Hit Rolls]].

Attacks made with a Torrent weapon automatically hit.

## Interactions

- [[Hit Rolls]] is the attack-sequence step whose normal handling this ability changes.
- [[Attack System]] is the shared attack pipeline that receives the automatic-hit result.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Torrent inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- [[Core Abilities]] provides the wider attack-facing ability index.

## Restrictions

- Torrent changes how attacks hit; it does not redefine wound rolls, saves, or damage.
- This article does not restate any downstream attack steps.
