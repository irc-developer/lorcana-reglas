# Assault

## Summary

Assault is the weapon ability that allows units with one or more qualifying weapons to use assault shooting.

## Definition

Units with one or more Assault weapons can use assault shooting.

## Rules

Assault grants access to [[Assault Shooting]].

It does not itself redefine the shooting-mode procedure that follows.

## Interactions

- [[Assault Shooting]] is the shooting mode enabled by this weapon ability.
- [[Shooting System]] owns the phase-level procedure that consumes this ability.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Assault inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- [[Core Abilities]] provides the wider attack-facing ability index.

## Restrictions

- Assault does not itself resolve attack declaration or attack resolution.
- This page is distinct from [[Assault Shooting]], which is the enabled shooting mode rather than the weapon ability.
