# Hazardous

## Summary

Hazardous is the weapon ability that causes a hazard roll for each selected Hazardous weapon after the unit resolves its attacks.

## Definition

After a unit resolves attacks, make one hazard roll per selected Hazardous weapon.

## Rules

Hazardous is checked after the unit resolves attacks.

Make one [[Hazard Rolls|hazard roll]] per selected Hazardous weapon.
