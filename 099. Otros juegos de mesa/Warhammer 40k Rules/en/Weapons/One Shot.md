# One Shot

## Summary

One Shot is the weapon ability that limits a weapon to being selected once per battle.

## Definition

A weapon with One Shot can only be selected once per battle.

## Rules

One Shot limits how often the weapon can be selected.

Revived models do not regain spent One Shot weapons.

Newly added units do get their One Shot use.
