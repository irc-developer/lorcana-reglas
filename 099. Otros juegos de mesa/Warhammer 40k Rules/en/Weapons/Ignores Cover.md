# Ignores Cover

## Summary

Ignores Cover is the weapon ability that prevents the target from having the benefit of cover against that attack.

## Definition

The target cannot have the benefit of cover against that attack, including from rules that grant cover such as Stealth.

## Rules

Ignores Cover applies against the target of that attack.

The target cannot have the [[Benefit of Cover]] against that attack.

This also applies against rules that grant cover, such as Stealth.

## Interactions

- [[Benefit of Cover]] is the defensive state this ability prevents.
- [[Attack System]] is the shared attack pipeline where this defensive state would otherwise matter.

## Restrictions

- Ignores Cover applies only against that attack.
- This page does not redefine visibility, target selection, or the general cover rules beyond the stated prevention effect.
