# Lance

## Summary

Lance is the weapon ability that adds 1 to wound rolls if the attacking model's unit made a charge move this turn.

## Definition

Add 1 to wound rolls if the attacking model's unit made a charge move this turn.

## Rules

Lance modifies [[Wound Rolls]].

It applies only if the attacking model's unit made a [[Charge Move]] this turn.
