# Armour Penetration

## Summary

Armour Penetration is the weapon value that carries the weapon's save-modifying pressure into the saving-throw stage of attack resolution.

## Definition

Armour Penetration is the AP value on a weapon profile. It is the weapon characteristic that modifies save handling when an attack reaches the saving-throw stage.

## Rules

Armour Penetration is recorded as part of the weapon profile.

Its purpose is to carry the weapon's penetration value into the defender's saving-throw resolution.

The AP value matters only if the attack reaches the point where a saving throw is being resolved.

## Interactions

- [[Weapon Characteristics]] places Armour Penetration inside the wider weapon data model.
- [[Weapon Profile]] is the glossary surface where the AP value appears with the other weapon fields.
- [[Save Rolls]] is the downstream step that consumes Armour Penetration.

## Restrictions

- Armour Penetration does not select weapons or targets.
- Armour Penetration does not determine the number of attacks or the damage characteristic.
- This article does not replace the saving-throw procedure.
