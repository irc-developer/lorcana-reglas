# Devastating Wounds

## Summary

Devastating Wounds is the weapon ability that turns a critical wound into a mortal-wound outcome after the attack's normal damage is resolved.

## Definition

On a critical wound, the attack sequence ends and the target suffers mortal wounds equal to D after normal damage from those attacks is resolved.

## Rules

Devastating Wounds triggers on a critical wound.

After normal damage from those attacks is resolved, the target suffers mortal wounds equal to the weapon's D characteristic.

Each critical wound can damage at most one model.

Excess mortal wounds from that attack are lost.

## Restrictions

- Excess mortal wounds from the attack are lost.
