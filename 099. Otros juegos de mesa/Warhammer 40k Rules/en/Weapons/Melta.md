# Melta

## Summary

Melta is the weapon ability that increases the weapon's Damage characteristic within half range until the attacking unit's attacks are resolved.

## Definition

Within half range, add X to the weapon's D characteristic until the attacking unit's attacks are resolved.

## Rules

Melta checks whether the target was within half range in [[Select Targets]].

When the condition is met, add the listed value to the weapon's D characteristic until the attacking unit's attacks are resolved.

## Interactions

- [[Select Targets]] is the upstream step that fixes whether the target was within half range.
- [[Inflict Damage]] is the downstream step that consumes the modified Damage characteristic.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Melta inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- [[Core Abilities]] provides the wider attack-facing ability index.

## Restrictions

- Melta applies only within half range.
- The modifier lasts until the attacking unit's attacks are resolved.
- This page does not redefine the general damage or targeting procedures.
