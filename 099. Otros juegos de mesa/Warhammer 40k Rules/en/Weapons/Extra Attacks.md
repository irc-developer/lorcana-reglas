# Extra Attacks

## Summary

Extra Attacks is the weapon ability that lets a model make attacks with that weapon in addition to one other melee weapon if possible.

## Definition

Models with Extra Attacks weapons make attacks with them in addition to one other melee weapon if possible.

## Rules

Extra Attacks modifies how melee weapons are selected in [[Select Weapons]].

It allows attacks with that weapon in addition to one other melee weapon if possible.

## Interactions

- [[Select Weapons]] is the attack-declaration step that this ability modifies.
- [[Weapon Abilities]] places Extra Attacks inside the broader weapon-ability cluster.
- [[Core Abilities]] provides the wider attack-facing ability index.

## Restrictions

- Extra Attacks applies only as stated to attacks with that weapon.
- This page does not redefine the wider weapon-selection procedure beyond the stated addition.
