# Weapon Characteristic

## Summary

Weapon Characteristic is the glossary entry for an individual printed weapon value inside a weapon profile.

## Definition

A weapon characteristic is one of the printed weapon fields that define how a weapon behaves, such as Range, Attacks, BS or WS, Strength, Armour Penetration, or Damage.
