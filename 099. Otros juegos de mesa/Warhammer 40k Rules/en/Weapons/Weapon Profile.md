# Weapon Profile

## Summary

Weapon Profile is the glossary entry for the complete datasheet line that records a weapon's characteristics and weapon abilities together.

## Definition

A weapon profile is the datasheet entry that records a weapon's printed fields, including its characteristics and any weapon abilities listed for that weapon.
