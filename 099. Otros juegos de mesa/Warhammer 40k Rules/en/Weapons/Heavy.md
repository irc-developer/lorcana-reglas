# Heavy

## Summary

Heavy is the weapon ability that improves hit determination in your Shooting phase when the firing unit stayed sufficiently still.

## Definition

In your Shooting phase, add 1 to hit with a Heavy weapon if the unit is unengaged, was not set up this turn, and no model in it moved more than 3" that turn.

## Rules

Heavy applies only in your Shooting phase.

When its conditions are met, add 1 to hit with the Heavy weapon.

The unit must be unengaged, must not have been set up this turn, and no model in it may have moved more than 3" that turn.
