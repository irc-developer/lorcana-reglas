# Crushing Impact

## Summary

Crushing Impact is the core stratagem that lets a MONSTER or VEHICLE that ended a charge move risk mortal wounds to itself while inflicting mortal wounds on an engaged enemy unit.

## Definition

Crushing Impact is used in your Charge phase just after a friendly MONSTER or VEHICLE unit ends a charge move.

## Rules

Cost: 1CP.

Select one enemy unit engaged with your MONSTER or VEHICLE unit.

Select one model in your unit that is engaged with that enemy unit.

Roll a number of D6 equal to that model's Toughness characteristic.

Each result of 1 causes your unit to suffer 1 mortal wound.

Each result of 5+ causes the selected enemy unit to suffer 1 mortal wound, to a maximum of 6 mortal wounds per unit.

## Interactions

## Restrictions

- The selected friendly unit must be a MONSTER or VEHICLE unit.
- The mortal wounds inflicted on the enemy unit are capped at 6 for each unit.
