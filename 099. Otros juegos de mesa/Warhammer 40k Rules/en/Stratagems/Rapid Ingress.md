# Rapid Ingress

## Summary

Rapid Ingress is the core stratagem that lets one eligible friendly Strategic Reserves unit make an Ingress Move at the end of the opponent's Movement phase.

## Definition

Rapid Ingress is used at the end of the opponent's Movement phase on a friendly unit in Strategic Reserves, excluding AIRCRAFT.

## Rules

Cost: 1CP.

Target: one friendly unit in Strategic Reserves, excluding AIRCRAFT.

Effect: that unit makes an Ingress Move.

Restriction: this stratagem cannot be used during the first battle round.

## Interactions

- [[Ingress Move]] owns the movement procedure performed by the target unit.
- [[Strategic Reserves]] provides the target state.
- [[Using Stratagems]] governs CP spending.

## Restrictions

- Rapid Ingress cannot target AIRCRAFT.
- Rapid Ingress cannot be used during the first battle round.
