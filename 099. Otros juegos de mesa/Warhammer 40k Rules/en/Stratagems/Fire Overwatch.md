# Fire Overwatch

## Summary

Fire Overwatch is the stratagem that lets one friendly unengaged non-TITANIC unit shoot using Snap Shooting at the end of the opponent's Movement phase.

## Definition

At the end of the opponent's Movement phase, one friendly unengaged non-TITANIC unit can shoot using snap shooting.

## Rules

Fire Overwatch is a permission to shoot outside the normal Shooting phase sequence.

It is used at the end of the opponent's Movement phase.

One friendly unengaged non-TITANIC unit can shoot using [[Snap Shooting]].

## Interactions

- [[Movement Phase]] is the opponent-phase timing window in which this stratagem is used.
- [[Shooting Phase]] is the normal timing owner that this stratagem temporarily bypasses.
- [[Snap Shooting]] is the special shooting procedure consumed by this stratagem.
- [[Attack System]] is the downstream shared attack pipeline used once the permitted shooting begins resolving.

## Restrictions

- The unit must be friendly, unengaged, and non-TITANIC.
- Fire Overwatch is a permission to shoot outside the normal Shooting phase sequence.
- This page does not redefine the Snap Shooting procedure or the shared attack pipeline.
