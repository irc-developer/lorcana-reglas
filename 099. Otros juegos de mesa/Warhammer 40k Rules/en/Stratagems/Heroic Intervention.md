# Heroic Intervention

## Summary

Heroic Intervention is the core stratagem that lets an eligible friendly unit resolve a charge at the end of the opponent's Charge phase using Leap to Defend or Into the Fray.

## Definition

Heroic Intervention is used at the end of the opponent's Charge phase on an eligible friendly unengaged unit within 12 inches of one or more enemy units.

## Rules

Cost: 1CP, with an additional +1CP section for Into the Fray.

A VEHICLE unit can be selected only if it is a CHARACTER or WALKER unit.

The target unit resolves a charge.

Before making the charge roll, choose Leap to Defend or Into the Fray.

Leap to Defend only allows charge targets that made a charge move this phase and are within the maximum distance.

Into the Fray changes a charge roll greater than 6 after modifiers to 6 and allows charge targets within 6 inches of your unit and within the maximum distance.

## Interactions

- [[Charge]] owns the charge sequence resolved by this stratagem.
- [[Charge Move]] owns the movement procedure if the charge is made.
- [[Charge target]] is the target state created during the charge.

## Restrictions

- The selected unit must be unengaged and within 12 inches of one or more enemy units.
- Into the Fray uses the additional CP cost section.
- This stratagem does not bypass the charge sequence it tells you to resolve.
