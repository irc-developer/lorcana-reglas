# Smokescreen

## Summary

Smokescreen is the core stratagem that gives cover protection to a friendly SMOKE unit and to units obscured by that SMOKE unit during the opponent's Shooting phase.

## Definition

Smokescreen is used at the start of the opponent's Shooting phase on one friendly SMOKE unit.

## Rules

Cost: 1CP.

Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets the SMOKE unit, the target has the benefit of cover.

Until the end of the phase, each time an attack targets a unit that is not fully visible to the attacking model because of one or more models in the SMOKE unit, the target has the benefit of cover.

## Interactions

- [[Benefit of Cover]] owns the defensive effect granted by the stratagem.
- [[Shooting Phase]] provides the timing window.
- [[Using Stratagems]] governs CP spending and targeting.

## Restrictions

- This stratagem does not create Hidden or Obscuring terrain state.
- The second protection branch depends on visibility being blocked by models in the selected SMOKE unit.
