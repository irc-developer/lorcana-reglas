# Counteroffensive

## Summary

Counteroffensive is the core stratagem that gives an eligible friendly unit Fights First and forces it to be the next unit selected to fight.

## Definition

Counteroffensive is used in the Fight step of the opponent's Fight phase just after an enemy unit has resolved its attacks.

## Rules

Cost: 2CP.

Target: one friendly unit that is eligible to fight.

Until the end of the phase, that unit has Fights First.

That unit must be the next unit you select to fight.

## Interactions

- [[Fight Phase]] provides the timing window.
- [[Fight]] is the combat procedure affected by the forced next selection.
- [[Fights First]] is the state granted by the stratagem.

## Restrictions

- The target must be eligible to fight.
- The selected unit must be the next unit you select to fight.
