# Epic Challenge

## Summary

Epic Challenge is the Fight-phase stratagem that gives one CHARACTER model in a selected friendly CHARACTER unit Precision on its melee weapons until the end of the phase.

## Definition

In the Fight phase, just after a friendly CHARACTER unit is selected to fight, one CHARACTER model in that unit gains Precision on its melee weapons until end of phase.

## Rules

Epic Challenge is used in the Fight phase, just after a friendly CHARACTER unit is selected to fight.

One CHARACTER model in that unit gains [[Precision]] on its melee weapons until the end of the phase.

It applies to one selected CHARACTER model, not the whole unit.
