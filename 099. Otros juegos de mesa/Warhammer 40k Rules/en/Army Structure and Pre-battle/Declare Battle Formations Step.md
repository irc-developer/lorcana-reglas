# Declare Battle Formations Step

## Summary

Declare Battle Formations Step is the source-referenced pre-battle step for reserve placement, embarked transport setup, and aircraft reserve placement.

## Definition

The Declare Battle Formations step is a before-battle step referenced by Strategic Reserves, Transports, and Aircraft rules.

## Rules

During this step, eligible units can be placed in Strategic Reserves instead of being deployed on the battlefield.

During this step, units can start embarked within a friendly TRANSPORT model that has sufficient capacity for the whole unit.

During this step, all AIRCRAFT units must be placed in Strategic Reserves.
