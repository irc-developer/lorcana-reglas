# Missions and Battle Preparation

## Summary

Missions and Battle Preparation is the pre-battle framing article for selecting a mission, preparing armies, creating a battlefield, and establishing the conditions for victory.

In the new mission system, a player's chosen [[Force Dispositions|Force Disposition]] can also determine which mission context is used.

## Definition

A game of Warhammer 40,000 is played using a mission that tells players how to prepare the battle and what they must do to win.

Force Dispositions are mission-facing orders unlocked by [[Detachments]]. When a mission pack uses them, each player chooses one available Force Disposition for their army and compares it with the opponent's choice to determine the mission context.

## Rules

Before play, players use a mission to determine how the battle is prepared and how victory is achieved.

The mission instructs players how to muster armies and states the total size those armies should be.

Before playing, players also create a battlefield with terrain; the mission can define battlefield size, terrain volume, and terrain placement.

## Force Dispositions and Mission Determination

When using the new Force Disposition mission framework:

1. The army's selected detachments unlock one or more Force Dispositions.
2. The player chooses one available Force Disposition to represent the army for the battle.
3. The chosen Force Disposition is compared with the opponent's chosen Force Disposition.
4. That comparison determines the specific mission context or mission matchup.

If an army has access to several Force Dispositions, only the chosen Force Disposition governs mission determination. It is not automatically determined by the highest-cost detachment, by the first detachment chosen, or by the number of detachments of each category.

In casual games, the source indicates this choice can vary from battle to battle. In organised play, event packs commonly require a single Force Disposition to be locked when the list is submitted.

## Interactions

- [[Army]] provides the basic army concept consumed by mission preparation.
- [[Detachments]] unlock Force Dispositions.
- [[Force Dispositions]] can determine mission context when the mission pack uses that system.
- [[Terrain Objectives]] and other objective rules can be introduced by missions.
- [[Battle Round]] begins once pre-battle preparation has led into the live battle.
