# Resolve Pre-battle Abilities

## Summary

Resolve Pre-battle Abilities is the source-referenced timing step after Declare Battle Formations and before Begin the Battle.

## Definition

Resolve Pre-battle Abilities is the pre-battle timing step in which rules such as Scouts can resolve.

## Rules

Scouts X" resolves in this step if every model in the relevant unit has the ability.

Scout Move is made in this step.

The Core Rules appendix states that this step comes after Declare Battle Formations and before Begin the Battle.

## Interactions

- [[Scouts]] owns the ability that uses this step.
- [[Scout Move]] owns the movement procedure resolved during this step.
- [[Declare Battle Formations Step]] and [[Begin the Battle]] frame this step in the pre-battle sequence.

## Restrictions

- The current Core Rules source does not provide a complete list of all possible pre-battle abilities in this article.
- Do not treat a scout move as an in-battle move for transport embarkation.
