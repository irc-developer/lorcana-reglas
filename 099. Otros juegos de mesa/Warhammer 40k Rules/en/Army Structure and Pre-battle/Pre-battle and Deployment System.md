# Pre-battle and Deployment System

## Overview

Pre-battle and Deployment System is the domain MOC for the setup sequence that happens before the first battle round. It maps the mission-and-army framing concepts, preserves the placeholder procedures that remain structurally incomplete in the current source scope, and links this setup domain to the systems that consume faction, reserve, keyword, and attached-unit decisions.

## Scope

This domain covers the before-battle sequence, the mission-and-army framing concepts that belong to it, and the planned or placeholder procedures that govern deployment-stage setup before the first battle round begins.

It helps answer questions about what happens before live play starts, which setup decisions belong to this domain, and when a reader should move into reserves, attached units, keywords, abilities, or the battle sequence after the pre-battle stage ends.

## Domain Ownership

- Owner domain: Army Structure and Pre-battle.
- Publication role: organise the before-battle sequence, its army-structure framing concepts, and the placeholder deployment steps that remain source-gapped.
- Domain system role: provide the authoritative entry point and navigation surface for planned pre-battle and deployment-domain content.

## Domain Boundaries

Army Structure and Pre-battle owns:

- the planned pre-battle concepts Missions and Battle Preparation and Army Faction and Faction Keywords.
- the planned or placeholder procedures Muster Armies Step, Declare Battle Formations Step, Resolve Pre-battle Abilities, Begin the Battle, and Adding a New Unit to Your Army.

Army Structure and Pre-battle does not own:

- [[Strategic Reserves System]] ownership of reserve placement and arrival once that branch is entered.
- [[Attached Unit System]] ownership of attached-unit-specific interaction rules.
- [[Ability System]] and [[Keyword System]] ownership of ability and keyword semantics used during setup.
- [[Battle Sequence System]] ownership of the live battle-round timing spine that begins after pre-battle handling ends.
