# Begin the Battle

## Summary

Begin the Battle is the source-referenced transition point after pre-battle abilities and before the live battle sequence.

## Definition

Begin the Battle marks the transition from pre-battle handling into the battle sequence.

## Rules

The Core Rules appendix places Begin the Battle after the Resolve Pre-battle Abilities step.

After pre-battle handling is complete, the game proceeds into the battle-round structure defined by the mission and the Battle Sequence System.

## Interactions

- [[Resolve Pre-battle Abilities]] is the immediately preceding referenced step.
- [[Battle Round]] owns the live battle loop after the pre-battle stage ends.

## Restrictions

- The current Core Rules source references this step but does not fully define it as a standalone procedure in the extracted core material.
- Use mission instructions when they define additional start-of-battle requirements.
