# Army Faction and Faction Keywords

## Summary

Army Faction and Faction Keywords is the setup-facing article for the selected army faction and its relationship to faction keywords on datasheets.

## Definition

A unit’s faction abilities normally apply only if the selected army faction matches a faction keyword on that unit’s datasheet.

## Rules

Datasheets separate faction keywords from other keywords.

Faction keywords are used when deciding which models to include in an army.

Unless a rule states otherwise, a unit’s faction abilities apply only if the selected army faction matches a faction keyword on that unit’s datasheet.

## Interactions

- [[Faction Abilities]] owns the ability-family rule that checks the selected army faction.
- [[Keywords]] owns general keyword semantics.
- [[Datasheet System]] owns the datasheet structure that carries faction keywords.

## Restrictions

- This article does not list codex-specific faction abilities.
