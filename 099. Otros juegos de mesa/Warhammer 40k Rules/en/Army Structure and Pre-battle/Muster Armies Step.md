# Muster Armies Step

## Summary

Muster Armies Step is the source-referenced pre-battle step where army construction and leader/support attachment decisions are made.

## Definition

The Muster Armies step is referenced as the before-battle step in which Leader and Support units can be assigned to eligible bodyguard units.

## Rules

For each leader or support unit in your army, during this step you can select one friendly bodyguard unit that it can lead.

The selected leader or support unit then forms an attached unit with that bodyguard unit for the battle.

The army faction selected while mustering your army is relevant to faction abilities.

## Interactions

- [[Leader]] and [[Support]] point into this step for attached-unit formation.
