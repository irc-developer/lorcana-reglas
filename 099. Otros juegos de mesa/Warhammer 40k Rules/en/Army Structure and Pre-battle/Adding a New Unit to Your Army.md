# Adding a New Unit to Your Army

## Summary

Adding a New Unit to Your Army is the appendix rule for determining starting strength and weapon-use state when a rule adds a new unit during a battle.

## Definition

When a rule adds a new unit to your army during a battle, that unit’s starting strength is determined when it is added.

## Rules

The new unit’s starting strength is determined when the unit is added to your army.

Apart from that timing, starting strength is determined in the same way as for other units.

If a new unit is added to an army, all One Shot weapons in that unit can be selected to make attacks once per battle.

## Interactions

- [[Starting Strength and Half-strength]] owns the strength-state framework used by the new unit.
- [[One Shot]] owns the weapon-use rule that resets for newly added units.

## Restrictions

- This article applies only when a rule adds a new unit to an army during a battle.
- This article does not define the source rule that creates the new unit.
