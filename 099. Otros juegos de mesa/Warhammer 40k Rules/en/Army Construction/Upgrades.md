# Upgrades

## Summary

Upgrades are enhancement-tagged options that can be applied to non-Character units under the new army-construction system.

## Definition

Upgrade is a tag used on some enhancements. An Upgrade-tagged enhancement can be applied to up to three non-Character units while counting as a single enhancement choice, though the points cost is paid for each application.

## Rules

When an enhancement has the Upgrade tag:

- it can be applied to up to three non-Character units;
- it counts as a single enhancement choice;
- its points cost is paid individually for each unit that receives it.

## Interactions

- [[Enhancement Limit]] constrains how many enhancement choices are available.
- [[Detachments]] can provide enhancements that may include Upgrade-tagged options.
- [[Army Construction]] applies Upgrade choices while building the list.

## Restrictions

- This article does not define the text, points cost, or eligibility of any specific Upgrade.
- Specific Upgrade options require source-backed faction or detachment articles.
