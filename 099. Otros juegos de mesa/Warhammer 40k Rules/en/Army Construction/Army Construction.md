# Army Construction

## Summary

Army Construction is the list-building procedure used before the pre-battle sequence. It determines the army's size, detachment budget, unit limits, enhancement limits, and relevant detachment-derived outputs.

## Definition

Army Construction is the process of creating an army list before a game of Warhammer 40,000.

The process uses battle size, points total, Detachment Points, enhancement limits, unit limits, detachments, and any source-specific restrictions that apply to the selected army.

## Rules

### Battle Size

Choose or confirm the battle size for the game. The battle size defines the points total, the Detachment Points available, the enhancement limit, and the unit limit.

### Points Total

The points total is the maximum points value available for the army at that battle size.

### Detachments

Select detachments within the Detachment Points available for the battle size.

Detachments provide rules for the army and can provide enhancements, stratagems, and Force Dispositions.

### Limits

Apply the enhancement limit and unit limit for the battle size.

The unit limit is the maximum number of each unit that can be included in the army. The published note doubles this limit for BATTLELINE units.

## Interactions

- [[Battle Size]] determines the relevant construction limits.
- [[Detachment Points]] constrains detachment selection.
- [[Detachments]] provide list-building packages that can include stratagems, enhancements, and Force Dispositions.
- [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] begins after the army list exists.
- [[Strategic Reserves System]] uses the army's points limit for its reserve cap.
