# Battle Size

## Summary

Battle Size is the army-construction setting that determines the points total, available Detachment Points, enhancement limit, and unit limit for the game.

## Definition

Battle Size is the selected game size used to configure the army-building limits for a game.

## Rules

The published construction table currently includes:

| Battle Size | Points Total | Detachment Points | Enhancement Limit | Unit Limit |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Incursion | 1,000 | 2 | 2 | 2 |
| Strike Force | 2,000 | 3 | 4 | 3 |

The Unit Limit is doubled for BATTLELINE units.

## Interactions

- [[Detachment Points]] are read from the battle size.
- [[Enhancement Limit]] is read from the battle size.
- [[Unit Limit]] is read from the battle size.
- [[Army Construction]] uses battle size as its main configuration input.

## Restrictions

- This article records the source-backed published sizes currently available in the source article.
- Other mission packs or publications may add, remove, or override battle-size details.
