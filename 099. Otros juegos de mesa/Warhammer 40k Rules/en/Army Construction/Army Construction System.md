# Army Construction System

## Overview

Army Construction System is the entry point for building a Warhammer 40,000 army list before the pre-battle and deployment sequence begins.

This domain covers the list-building layer: choosing battle size, applying a points total, spending Detachment Points, observing enhancement and unit limits, and carrying forward list-building decisions into pre-battle setup.

## Scope

This domain answers questions about list creation rather than tabletop resolution.

It covers:

- battle size and points total;
- Detachment Points and detachment selection;
- enhancement and unit limits;
- Upgrade-tagged enhancements;
- Force Dispositions granted by detachments;
- which decisions are made while building the army list.

## Domain Ownership

Army Construction owns:

- [[Army Construction]] as the list-building umbrella.
- [[Battle Size]] as the game-size configuration node.
- [[Detachment Points]] as the list-building resource for selecting detachments.
- [[Detachments]] as the package of army rules selected during list building.
- [[Enhancement Limit]] as the limit on enhancement choices.
- [[Unit Limit]] as the limit on copies of each unit.
- [[Upgrades]] as the Upgrade-tagged enhancement branch.
- [[Force Dispositions]] as the mission-facing output granted by detachments.

Army Construction does not own:

- [[Command Point (CP)]], which is the in-game stratagem resource.
- [[Using Stratagems]], even though detachments can provide stratagems.
- [[Declare Battle Formations Step]], which belongs to pre-battle setup.
- [[Strategic Reserves System]], even though reserves use the army's points limit.
- [[Attached Unit System]], even though some join decisions are made while building the list.

## Related Systems

- [[Pre-battle and Deployment System]] consumes the completed army list once the game moves into setup.
- [[Datasheet System]] supplies units, characteristics, keywords, weapons, wargear options, and abilities used while constructing a list.
- [[Keyword System]] supports faction and unit restrictions.
- [[Stratagem System]] is related because detachments can provide stratagems, but it does not own detachment selection.
- [[Attached Unit System]] is related because Leader and Support attachment decisions can be made while building the list.
