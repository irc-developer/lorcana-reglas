# Enhancement Limit

## Summary

Enhancement Limit is the army-construction limit on enhancement choices available at a given battle size.

## Definition

Enhancement Limit is the number of enhancement choices available when building an army for a battle size.

## Rules

Published examples:

| Battle Size | Enhancement Limit |
| --- | ---: |
| Incursion | 2 |
| Strike Force | 4 |

Detachments can provide enhancements.

Some enhancements can have the Upgrade tag, which changes how they can be applied to non-Character units.

## Interactions

- [[Battle Size]] determines the enhancement limit.
- [[Detachments]] can provide enhancements.
- [[Upgrades]] are a special enhancement branch for non-Character units.

## Restrictions

- This article records the limit structure, not the rules text of individual enhancements.
- Individual enhancement costs and restrictions require source-backed detachment or faction articles.
