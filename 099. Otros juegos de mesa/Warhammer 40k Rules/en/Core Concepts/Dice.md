# Dice

## Summary

Dice is the foundational Core Concepts article for the game's D6-based notation, target-number language, and derived forms such as D3 and multi-die totals.

## Definition

The game uses D6-based notation including fixed target numbers, ranges, multiple dice, D3, and additive modifiers.

## Rules

Results written as 2+, 3+, and similar values mean equal to or greater than the shown number.

Expressions such as 2D6 and 3D6 mean that many D6 are rolled and their results are added together.

D3 means half of a D6 result, rounded up.

This article establishes the shared dice notation used by other rules. It does not define any specific roll family by itself.

## Interactions

- [[Hit Rolls]], [[Wound Rolls]], and [[Save Rolls]] use this notation for the core attack sequence.
- [[Hazard Rolls]] uses the shared D6 roll framework for its support-risk check.
- [[Advance Move]] uses this article's D6 notation to extend a unit's move distance.
- [[Charge]] uses this article's multi-die notation when resolving the charge roll.
- [[Battle-shock Rolls]] consumes this article through the wider roll framework used by Leadership-based checks.

## Restrictions

- This article does not define when a specific roll is made or what success and failure mean for a downstream rule.
- This article does not replace rule-specific instructions that modify the number of dice rolled or how results are interpreted.
- If a rule states a bespoke dice method, that specific method takes precedence.
