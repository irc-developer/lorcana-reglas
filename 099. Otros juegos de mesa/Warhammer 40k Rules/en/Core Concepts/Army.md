# Army

## Summary

Army is the core-concepts article for the collection of units and models commanded by one player in a game of Warhammer 40,000.

## Definition

An army is the collection of units of models commanded by a player.

## Rules

Each player controls all models in their own army.

Friendly units and models are in your army; enemy units and models are in your opponent's army.

When a rule refers to the controlling player, it means the player who controls the models affected by that rule.
