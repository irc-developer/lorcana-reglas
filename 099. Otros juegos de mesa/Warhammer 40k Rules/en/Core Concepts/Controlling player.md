# Controlling player

## Summary

Controlling player is the glossary term for the player who controls the models affected by a rule.

## Definition

The controlling player is the player who controls the models being affected by the rule in question.

## Rules

Each player controls all models in their own army.

Some timing exceptions use the controlling player of a selected unit to determine who is the active player until that move or attack sequence ends.

## Interactions

- [[Army]] defines the army-control baseline.
- [[Units and Models]] provides the unit/model context for controlled models.
- [[Active Player and Opposing Player]] explains when controlling-player identity affects active-player timing.

## Restrictions

- Controlling player is ownership authority; active player is timing authority. They often match, but not always.
