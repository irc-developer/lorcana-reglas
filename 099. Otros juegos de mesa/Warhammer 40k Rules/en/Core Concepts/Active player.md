# Active player

## Summary

Active player is the glossary term for the player who currently has active timing authority.

## Definition

The active player is one of the two always-present player roles defined by [[Active Player and Opposing Player]].

## Rules

The active player changes as the battle sequence and unit-selection exceptions require.

When a player becomes the active player, the other player becomes the opposing player.
