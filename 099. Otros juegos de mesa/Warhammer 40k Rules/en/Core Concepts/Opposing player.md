# Opposing player

## Summary

Opposing player is the glossary term for the player who is not currently the active player.

## Definition

The opposing player is the counterpart role to the active player.

## Rules

When one player is the active player, the other player is the opposing player.

The opposing-player role changes whenever the active-player role changes.

- [[Active Player and Opposing Player]] owns the complete rule meaning.

## Restrictions

- Opposing player is not a synonym for opponent in all prose; it is a timing role.
