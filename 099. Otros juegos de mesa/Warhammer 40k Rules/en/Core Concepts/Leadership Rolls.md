# Leadership Rolls

## Summary

Leadership Rolls is the core-concepts article for the 2D6 roll used to test a unit against one or more Leadership characteristics.

## Definition

A leadership roll is made by rolling 2D6 for a unit and comparing the result to Leadership characteristics in that unit.

## Rules

The roll succeeds if the 2D6 result is equal to or greater than one or more Leadership characteristics in that unit.

The roll fails if the result is lower than all relevant Leadership characteristics in that unit.

The rule that instructed the roll defines what success or failure does.

## Interactions

- [[Dice]] defines the D6 and 2D6 notation consumed by this roll.
- [[Battle-shock Rolls]] are a specific use of leadership rolls.
- [[Command Phase Battle-shock]] is the main phase-level consumer of battle-shock rolls.

## Restrictions

- This article defines the roll structure only; it does not define the consequences of every rule that calls for a leadership roll.
