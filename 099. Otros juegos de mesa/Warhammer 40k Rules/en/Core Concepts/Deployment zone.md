# Deployment zone

## Summary

[[Deployment zone]] is the mission-defined area used by deployment, reserve arrival, and several pre-battle positioning rules.

## Definition

Mission-defined area relevant to deployment, Scouts, Infiltrators, Deep Strike limitations, and reserve arrivals.
