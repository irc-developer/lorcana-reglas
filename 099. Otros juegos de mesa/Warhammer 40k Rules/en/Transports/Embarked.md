# Embarked

## Summary

Embarked is the glossary state for a unit that is inside a TRANSPORT and not on the battlefield.

## Definition

A unit is embarked while it has been placed to one side inside a TRANSPORT and is not on the battlefield.

## Rules

Embarked units occupy transport capacity.

Embarked units can disembark only when a rule allows them to do so.

A unit embarked within a TRANSPORT is not on the battlefield.

## Interactions

- [[Embarking]] is the procedure that creates the embarked state after the battle begins.
- [[Disembarking]] is the umbrella exit rule.
- [[Transport Capacity]] limits which units and models can be embarked.

## Restrictions

- This glossary entry does not define every consequence of not being on the battlefield.
