# Transports

## Summary

Transports is the umbrella article for TRANSPORT models that carry friendly models across the battlefield.

## Definition

A TRANSPORT model can carry friendly models as passengers according to its transport capacity and datasheet restrictions.

## Rules

More than one unit can be embarked within the same TRANSPORT model if the transport has enough remaining capacity.

Before the battle, eligible units can start embarked in a friendly TRANSPORT with sufficient capacity.

After the battle begins, units use [[Embarking]] and [[Disembarking]] rules to enter and exit transports.

## Interactions

- [[Transport Capacity]] defines how many and what kind of models can embark.
- [[Embarking]] covers entering a transport after the battle begins.
- [[Disembarking]] covers leaving a transport during the Movement phase or after the transport is destroyed.
- [[Embarked]] is the glossary state for a unit inside a transport.

## Restrictions

- This article is the domain umbrella and does not replace the specific embark or disembark procedures.
